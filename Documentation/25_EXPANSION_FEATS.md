# Expansion Feats Reference

Feats from Xanathar's Guide to Everything (XGtE) and Tasha's Cauldron of Everything (TCoE). Alphabetical within each source.

---

## Tasha's Cauldron of Everything Feats

These are available to any character meeting the prerequisites. TCoE feats are widely considered some of the best in the game.

### Artificer Initiate
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - Learn one Artificer cantrip (INT-based)
  - Learn one 1st-level Artificer spell. Cast it once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots if you have them. INT is the casting ability.
  - Gain proficiency with one type of artisan's tools

### Chef
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to CON or WIS
  - Gain proficiency with cook's utensils (or expertise if already proficient)
  - During a short rest, you can cook special food. Creatures who eat it regain 1d8 extra HP when spending Hit Dice to heal (up to PB creatures)
  - During a long rest, you can prepare PB treats. A creature that eats a treat (bonus action) gains PB temporary HP. Treats last until next long rest

### Crusher
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to STR or CON
  - Once per turn, when you hit with bludgeoning damage, you can move the target 5 ft to an unoccupied space (target must be no more than one size larger than you)
  - When you score a critical hit with bludgeoning damage, all attack rolls against that target have advantage until the start of your next turn

### Eldritch Adept
- **Prerequisite**: Spellcasting or Pact Magic feature
- **Benefits**:
  - Learn one Eldritch Invocation from the Warlock list
  - Must meet the invocation's prerequisites (level requirements still apply, but you can ignore Pact Boon requirements)
  - Can replace the invocation whenever you gain a level

### Fey Touched
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to INT, WIS, or CHA
  - Learn Misty Step and one 1st-level Divination or Enchantment spell. Cast each once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: the ability increased by this feat
  - Top picks for the 1st-level spell: Bless, Gift of Alacrity, Hex, Hunter's Mark, Bane, Command

One of the best feats in 5e. +1 to a key stat + two free spells + Misty Step.

### Fighting Initiate
- **Prerequisite**: Proficiency with a martial weapon
- **Benefits**:
  - Learn one Fighting Style from the Fighter list
  - Can replace it when you gain an ASI

### Gunner
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to DEX
  - Proficiency with firearms
  - No disadvantage on ranged attacks within 5 ft of a hostile creature
  - Ignore the loading property of firearms

### Metamagic Adept
- **Prerequisite**: Spellcasting or Pact Magic feature
- **Benefits**:
  - Learn two Metamagic options from the Sorcerer list
  - Gain 2 sorcery points (used only for Metamagic — can't convert to spell slots)
  - Sorcery points recharge on long rest
  - If you already have sorcery points, these add to your pool

### Piercer
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to STR or DEX
  - Once per turn, when you hit with piercing damage, you can reroll one of the damage dice and use the new roll
  - When you score a critical hit with piercing damage, roll one additional damage die

### Poisoner
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - Ignore resistance to poison damage
  - As an action, apply poison to a weapon or 3 pieces of ammunition. The poison lasts 1 minute or until you hit. Target makes CON save DC 14 or takes 2d8 poison damage and is poisoned until end of your next turn
  - Proficiency with the poisoner's kit. During a long rest, create a number of poison doses equal to PB using the kit (50 gp per dose of materials)

### Shadow Touched
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to INT, WIS, or CHA
  - Learn Invisibility and one 1st-level Illusion or Necromancy spell. Cast each once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: the ability increased by this feat
  - Good 1st-level picks: Inflict Wounds, Cause Fear, Silent Image, Disguise Self

Solid feat. Invisibility once per day is always useful.

### Skill Expert
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to any ability score
  - Gain proficiency in one skill
  - Choose one skill you're proficient in — gain expertise (double PB) in that skill

Best general-purpose skill feat. +1 to any stat, a new skill, and expertise.

### Slasher
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to STR or DEX
  - Once per turn, when you hit with slashing damage, reduce the target's speed by 10 ft until the start of your next turn
  - When you score a critical hit with slashing damage, the target has disadvantage on all attack rolls until the start of your next turn

### Telekinetic
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to INT, WIS, or CHA
  - Learn Mage Hand cantrip (if you already know it, range increases by 30 ft). The hand is invisible. You can cast it without verbal or somatic components.
  - As a bonus action, shove a creature within 30 ft of you 5 ft toward or away from you. The creature makes a STR save (DC 8 + PB + casting ability mod) or is moved. Can target willing creatures (auto-succeed). This costs no action on your part beyond the bonus action.

The bonus action shove is the real value — works every turn, no resource cost.

### Telepathic
- **Prerequisite**: None
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to INT, WIS, or CHA
  - You can speak telepathically to any creature within 60 ft. The creature doesn't need to share a language. It can't respond telepathically unless it has that ability.
  - Cast Detect Thoughts once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: the ability increased by this feat.

## Xanathar's Guide to Everything Feats

These are all racial feats — each requires a specific race.

### Bountiful Luck
- **Prerequisite**: Halfling
- **Benefits**: When an ally within 30 ft rolls a natural 1 on an attack, check, or save, you can use your reaction to let them reroll the die. They must use the new roll. Extends Lucky to your entire party.

### Dragon Fear
- **Prerequisite**: Dragonborn
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to STR, CON, or CHA
  - Instead of exhaling destructive energy with your Breath Weapon, you can roar. Each creature of your choice within 30 ft makes WIS save (DC = Breath Weapon DC) or is frightened for 1 minute. Target can repeat the save at end of each turn.

### Dragon Hide
- **Prerequisite**: Dragonborn
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to STR, CON, or CHA
  - Retractable claws — unarmed strikes deal 1d4 + STR slashing
  - Natural armor — AC = 13 + DEX modifier when not wearing armor

### Drow High Magic
- **Prerequisite**: Elf (Drow)
- **Benefits**:
  - Cast Detect Magic at will without a spell slot
  - Cast Levitate and Dispel Magic each once per long rest without a spell slot. CHA is the casting ability.

### Dwarf Fortitude
- **Prerequisite**: Dwarf
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to CON
  - When you take the Dodge action, you can spend one Hit Die to heal yourself (roll the die + CON modifier)

### Elven Accuracy
- **Prerequisite**: Elf or Half-Elf
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to DEX, INT, WIS, or CHA
  - When you have advantage on an attack roll using DEX, INT, WIS, or CHA, you can reroll one of the dice once. Effectively "super-advantage" — roll 3 dice, pick the highest.

One of the strongest racial feats. Massively increases crit chance (from ~9.75% with advantage to ~14.3% with triple advantage). Pairs extremely well with Hexblade, Samurai, any build that generates reliable advantage.

### Fade Away
- **Prerequisite**: Gnome
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to DEX or INT
  - When you take damage, you can use your reaction to turn invisible until the end of your next turn (or until you attack, deal damage, or force a save). Once per short or long rest.

### Fey Teleportation
- **Prerequisite**: Elf (High)
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to INT or CHA
  - Learn Sylvan
  - Cast Misty Step once per short or long rest without a spell slot

### Flames of Phlegethos
- **Prerequisite**: Tiefling
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to INT or CHA
  - When you cast a spell that deals fire damage, you can reroll any 1s on the fire damage dice. Must use the new roll.
  - When you cast a spell that deals fire damage, you can wreath yourself in flames until the end of your next turn. Creatures within 5 ft that hit you with a melee attack take 1d4 fire damage.

### Infernal Constitution
- **Prerequisite**: Tiefling
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to CON
  - Resistance to cold and poison damage (stacks with fire resistance from Tiefling)
  - Advantage on saves against being poisoned

### Orcish Fury
- **Prerequisite**: Half-Orc
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to STR or CON
  - Once per short or long rest, when you hit with a weapon attack, roll one additional weapon damage die and add it to the damage
  - When you use Relentless Endurance (drop to 1 HP), you can use your reaction to make one weapon attack

### Prodigy
- **Prerequisite**: Human, Half-Elf, or Half-Orc
- **Benefits**:
  - Gain one skill proficiency
  - Gain one tool proficiency
  - Learn one language
  - Choose one skill you're proficient in — gain expertise (double PB) in that skill

### Second Chance
- **Prerequisite**: Halfling
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to DEX, CON, or CHA
  - When a creature hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to force it to reroll. It must use the new roll. Once per short or long rest.

### Squat Nimbleness
- **Prerequisite**: Dwarf or Small race
- **Benefits**:
  - +1 to STR or DEX
  - +5 ft walking speed
  - Proficiency in Acrobatics or Athletics
  - Advantage on Athletics or Acrobatics checks to escape grapples

### Wood Elf Magic
- **Prerequisite**: Elf (Wood)
- **Benefits**:
  - Learn one Druid cantrip (WIS-based)
  - Cast Longstrider and Pass Without Trace each once per long rest without a spell slot. WIS is the casting ability.

Pass Without Trace once per day is the standout — +10 to party Stealth checks.

## MotM Notes on Racial Feats

With MotM's race revisions, some XGtE racial feats may need DM adjudication for revised races. The feats still exist but reference mechanics from original race versions. Most DMs allow them with minor adaptation. Key conflicts:
- Dragonborn feats reference Breath Weapon mechanics that changed in Fizban's Treasury of Dragons
- Some feat prerequisites reference subraces that MotM consolidated
- Generally: if your table uses MotM races, check with the DM before taking XGtE racial feats
