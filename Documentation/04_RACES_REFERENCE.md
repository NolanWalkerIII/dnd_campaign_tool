# Races Reference

Playable races from the 5e SRD and PHB. Each entry lists ability score increases, size, speed, traits, and languages. Racial traits are applied during character creation and are generally permanent.

## SRD Races (Full Details)

### Human

- **ASI**: +1 to all ability scores
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Languages**: Common + one extra
- **Traits**: None beyond the ASI spread

The +1 to everything is generically good. Variant Human (PHB, widely used) is more interesting:

**Variant Human** (PHB, not SRD but nearly universally allowed):
- **ASI**: +1 to two different ability scores
- **Feat**: One feat at level 1
- **Skill**: One additional skill proficiency
- **Languages**: Common + one extra

Variant Human is one of the strongest race choices because feats at level 1 open up builds that normally don't come online until level 4.

### Elf

- **ASI**: +2 DEX
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Darkvision**: 60 ft
- **Keen Senses**: Proficiency in Perception
- **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charmed, immune to magical sleep
- **Trance**: 4 hours of semiconscious rest replaces 8 hours of sleep (still need a long rest's full duration for spell recovery)
- **Languages**: Common, Elvish

**High Elf** (SRD subrace):
- **ASI**: +1 INT
- **Cantrip**: One wizard cantrip (INT-based)
- **Extra Language**: One additional language

**Wood Elf**:
- **ASI**: +1 WIS
- **Fleet of Foot**: 35 ft speed
- **Mask of the Wild**: Hide when lightly obscured by natural phenomena (foliage, rain, mist, snow)

**Dark Elf (Drow)**:
- **ASI**: +1 CHA
- **Superior Darkvision**: 120 ft
- **Sunlight Sensitivity**: Disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks in direct sunlight. Significant downside.
- **Drow Magic**: Dancing Lights cantrip, Faerie Fire at level 3 (1/day), Darkness at level 5 (1/day). CHA-based.
- **Drow Weapon Training**: Proficiency with rapiers, shortswords, hand crossbows

### Dwarf

- **ASI**: +2 CON
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 25 ft (not reduced by heavy armor)
- **Darkvision**: 60 ft
- **Dwarven Resilience**: Advantage on saves vs. poison, resistance to poison damage
- **Dwarven Combat Training**: Proficiency with battleaxe, handaxe, light hammer, warhammer
- **Tool Proficiency**: One of smith's tools, brewer's supplies, mason's tools
- **Stonecunning**: Double proficiency bonus on INT (History) checks related to stonework
- **Languages**: Common, Dwarvish

**Hill Dwarf**:
- **ASI**: +1 WIS
- **Dwarven Toughness**: +1 HP per level. Simple but effective.

**Mountain Dwarf**:
- **ASI**: +2 STR (total +2 STR, +2 CON — only race with two +2s)
- **Dwarven Armor Training**: Proficiency with light and medium armor

### Halfling

- **ASI**: +2 DEX
- **Size**: Small
- **Speed**: 25 ft
- **Lucky**: Reroll natural 1s on attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws. Must use the new roll. One of the best racial traits in the game.
- **Brave**: Advantage on saves vs. frightened
- **Halfling Nimbleness**: Move through the space of any creature of a size larger than yours
- **Languages**: Common, Halfling

**Lightfoot Halfling**:
- **ASI**: +1 CHA
- **Naturally Stealthy**: Hide behind a creature at least one size larger than you

**Stout Halfling**:
- **ASI**: +1 CON
- **Stout Resilience**: Advantage on saves vs. poison, resistance to poison damage

### Gnome

- **ASI**: +2 INT
- **Size**: Small
- **Speed**: 25 ft
- **Darkvision**: 60 ft
- **Gnome Cunning**: Advantage on INT, WIS, and CHA saving throws against magic. Extremely strong defensive trait.
- **Languages**: Common, Gnomish

**Rock Gnome**:
- **ASI**: +1 CON
- **Artificer's Lore**: Double proficiency on INT (History) checks for magic items, alchemical objects, or technological devices
- **Tinker**: Proficiency with tinker's tools. Can create small clockwork devices.

**Forest Gnome**:
- **ASI**: +1 DEX
- **Natural Illusionist**: Minor Illusion cantrip (INT-based)
- **Speak with Small Beasts**: Communicate simple ideas with Small or smaller beasts

### Half-Elf

- **ASI**: +2 CHA, +1 to two other ability scores
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Darkvision**: 60 ft
- **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charmed, immune to magical sleep
- **Skill Versatility**: Proficiency in two skills of your choice
- **Languages**: Common, Elvish, one extra

One of the strongest race choices in the game due to flexible ASIs and two free skills. Natural fit for any CHA-based class.

### Half-Orc

- **ASI**: +2 STR, +1 CON
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Darkvision**: 60 ft
- **Menacing**: Proficiency in Intimidation
- **Relentless Endurance**: Drop to 1 HP instead of 0 once per long rest. Doesn't work against instant-kill effects.
- **Savage Attacks**: On melee critical hit, roll one additional weapon damage die. Stacks well with Barbarian's Brutal Critical.
- **Languages**: Common, Orc

### Dragonborn

- **ASI**: +2 STR, +1 CHA
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Draconic Ancestry**: Choose a dragon type. Determines breath weapon damage type and resistance.
- **Breath Weapon**: Action to exhale in a line or cone (depends on type). 2d6 damage scaling to 5d6 at level 16. DEX or CON save for half. Uses = 1 per short rest.
- **Damage Resistance**: Resistance to your chosen dragon type's damage.
- **Languages**: Common, Draconic

| Dragon | Damage Type | Breath Shape |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| Black | Acid | 5×30 ft line (DEX save) |
| Blue | Lightning | 5×30 ft line (DEX save) |
| Brass | Fire | 5×30 ft line (DEX save) |
| Bronze | Lightning | 5×30 ft line (DEX save) |
| Copper | Acid | 5×30 ft line (DEX save) |
| Gold | Fire | 15 ft cone (DEX save) |
| Green | Poison | 15 ft cone (CON save) |
| Red | Fire | 15 ft cone (DEX save) |
| Silver | Cold | 15 ft cone (CON save) |
| White | Cold | 15 ft cone (CON save) |

**No darkvision.** Dragonborn are one of the few PHB races without it.

### Tiefling

- **ASI**: +2 CHA, +1 INT
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Darkvision**: 60 ft
- **Hellish Resistance**: Resistance to fire damage
- **Infernal Legacy**: Thaumaturgy cantrip. Hellish Rebuke at level 3 (1/day as 2nd-level). Darkness at level 5 (1/day). CHA-based.
- **Languages**: Common, Infernal

## Tasha's Cauldron of Everything — Custom Origin

Widely adopted optional rule: any race can reassign ASIs to different ability scores (+2/+1 or +1/+1/+1). Also allows swapping skill proficiencies and languages. If the table uses Tasha's rules, race choice becomes more about traits than ASIs.

---

## Race Selection Quick Guide for LLM

When helping a player pick a race, consider:
1. **What class are they playing?** Match primary ASI to primary ability.
2. **Do they want darkvision?** Most races have it. Human, Halfling, and Dragonborn notably don't.
3. **Defensive traits matter.** Halfling Lucky, Gnome Cunning, and Half-Orc Relentless Endurance are all strong.
4. **Variant Human** is the default pick for optimized builds due to the level 1 feat.
5. **If using Tasha's custom origin**, race selection is purely about traits. +2 CHA on a Dragonborn is no longer locked to STR.
