# Tasha's Cauldron of Everything — Optional Class Features

Optional features from TCoE that replace or supplement existing PHB class features. DM must approve these at the table. These are widely adopted and considered semi-standard at most tables.

---

## Universal Rules

### Proficiency Swaps
At any level that grants an ASI, a character can replace one skill proficiency with another skill proficiency, or one tool proficiency with another tool proficiency. The new proficiency must be from the class's available list.

### Cantrip Replacement
Any class that learns cantrips can swap one cantrip for another from the same class list whenever they gain a level in that class.

---

## Barbarian

### Primal Knowledge (Level 3, 10)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
Gain proficiency in one skill from: Animal Handling, Athletics, Intimidation, Nature, Perception, Survival. Gain a second at level 10.

### Instinctive Pounce (Level 7)
*Replaces nothing (supplements Feral Instinct).*
When you enter rage, you can move up to half your speed as part of the same bonus action. This movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks.

---

## Bard

### Additional Bard Spells
Adds to the Bard spell list: Command, Color Spray, Mirror Image, Enlarge/Reduce, Aid, Slow, Phantasmal Killer, Rary's Telepathic Bond, Heroes' Feast, Prismatic Spray, Antipathy/Sympathy, Prismatic Wall.

### Magical Inspiration (Level 2)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
When a creature uses a Bardic Inspiration die, if they use it on a damage roll or healing roll (not just attacks/saves/checks), they add the die to the total. Expands the use cases for Bardic Inspiration significantly.

### Bardic Versatility (Level 4+)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
At any level granting an ASI, you can swap one cantrip for another from the Bard list, OR swap one Expertise skill for another skill you're proficient in.

---

## Cleric

### Additional Cleric Spells
Adds to the Cleric spell list: Aura of Vitality, Spirit Shroud, Aura of Life, Summon Celestial, Sunbeam, Sunburst, Power Word Heal.

### Harness Divine Power (Level 2)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
As a bonus action, expend one use of Channel Divinity to recover an expended spell slot. Slot level must be equal to or less than half your proficiency bonus (rounded up). Can use a number of times equal to proficiency bonus per long rest.

| Proficiency Bonus | Max Slot Level Recovered |
|---|---|
| +2 | 1st |
| +3 | 2nd |
| +4 | 2nd |
| +5 | 3rd |
| +6 | 3rd |

### Cantrip Versatility (Level 4+)
At any ASI level, swap one Cleric cantrip for another.

### Blessed Strikes (Level 8)
*Replaces Divine Strike OR Potent Spellcasting (your choice, permanent).*
When you deal damage with a cantrip or weapon attack, you can add 1d8 radiant damage once per turn. Consolidates the two domain options into one flexible feature — works for both weapon clerics and caster clerics.

---

## Druid

### Additional Druid Spells
Adds to the Druid spell list: Augury, Continual Flame, Enlarge/Reduce, Aura of Vitality, Elemental Weapon, Revivify, Divination, Fire Shield, Cone of Cold, Flesh to Stone, Symbol.

### Wild Companion (Level 2)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
Expend one use of Wild Shape to cast Find Familiar without material components. The familiar is a fey (not beast) and disappears after a number of hours equal to half your Druid level (rounded down). Standard familiar rules apply. Gives Druids a scout/utility familiar without requiring the spell.

### Cantrip Versatility (Level 4+)
At any ASI level, swap one Druid cantrip for another.

---

## Fighter

### New Fighting Styles
These are added to the Fighter's available Fighting Style options at level 1:

**Blind Fighting**: You have blindsight with a range of 10 ft. Within that range, you can see anything that isn't behind total cover, even if you're blinded or in darkness. Also works against invisible creatures within 10 ft.

**Interception**: When a creature you can see hits a target within 5 ft of you (other than you) with an attack, you can use your reaction to reduce the damage by 1d10 + your proficiency bonus. Must be wielding a shield or a simple/martial weapon.

**Superior Technique**: Learn one Battle Master maneuver. Gain one superiority die (d6). This die is used to fuel the maneuver and is regained on short/long rest. If you already have superiority dice, this just adds one more.

**Thrown Weapon Fighting**: +2 damage with thrown weapons. Also lets you draw a thrown weapon as part of the attack.

**Unarmed Fighting**: Unarmed strikes deal 1d6 + STR (1d8 if both hands free). At the start of your turn, you can deal 1d4 bludgeoning to one creature you're grappling.

### Martial Versatility (Level 4+)
At any ASI level, replace one Fighting Style with another, OR replace one Battle Master maneuver with another.

## Monk

### Dedicated Weapon (Level 2)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
At the end of a short or long rest, touch one weapon you're proficient with that lacks the heavy or special property. It counts as a monk weapon until you use this feature again. Lets Monks use longswords, battleaxes, etc. as monk weapons.

### Ki-Fueled Attack (Level 3)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
If you spend at least 1 ki point as part of your action on your turn, you can make one unarmed strike or monk weapon attack as a bonus action. This gives a bonus action attack even when using ki features like Stunning Strike that don't normally grant one.

### Quickened Healing (Level 4)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
As an action, spend 2 ki points to regain hit points equal to one roll of your Martial Arts die + your proficiency bonus. Not efficient but gives out-of-combat self-healing.

### Focused Aim (Level 5)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
When you miss with an attack roll, you can spend 1 to 3 ki points to increase the roll by 2 per ki point spent. Can turn a miss into a hit. You can decide to use this after seeing the roll but before the DM declares hit or miss. Does not work on natural 1s.

---

## Paladin

### Additional Paladin Spells
Adds to the Paladin spell list: Gentle Repose, Prayer of Healing, Warding Bond, Spirit Shroud, Summon Celestial.

### Additional Fighting Styles
Paladin gains access to Blind Fighting, Interception, Blessed Warrior (see below), and Thrown Weapon Fighting.

**Blessed Warrior**: Learn two Cleric cantrips. They count as Paladin spells for you. Replace one cantrip at any ASI level. Gives Paladins ranged options (Toll the Dead, Sacred Flame).

### Harness Divine Power (Level 3)
Same as Cleric version — bonus action, Channel Divinity for a spell slot ≤ half PB rounded up, PB uses per long rest.

### Martial Versatility (Level 4+)
At any ASI level, replace one Fighting Style with another.

## Ranger

### Deft Explorer (Levels 1, 6, 10)
*Replaces Natural Explorer (PHB). Choose Deft Explorer if you want the TCoE version.*

**Canny (Level 1)**: Choose one skill you're proficient in. Double proficiency bonus for that skill (Expertise). Also learn two languages.

**Roving (Level 6)**: Walking speed +5 ft. Gain climbing speed and swimming speed equal to walking speed.

**Tireless (Level 10)**: As an action, gain temporary HP equal to 1d8 + WIS modifier. PB uses per long rest. Also, whenever you finish a short rest, reduce your exhaustion level by 1.

### Favored Foe (Level 1)
*Replaces Favored Enemy (PHB). Choose Favored Foe if you want the TCoE version.*
When you hit a creature with an attack roll, you can mark it as your favored foe. First time each turn you deal damage to it, it takes extra 1d4 damage. Lasts 1 minute, requires concentration. PB uses per long rest. Damage scales to 1d6 at level 6 and 1d8 at level 14.

Note: requires concentration, which competes with Hunter's Mark and other Ranger spells. This is the main drawback vs. Hunter's Mark.

### Additional Ranger Spells
Adds: Entangle, Searing Smite, Aid, Gust of Wind, Magic Weapon, Summon Beast, Elemental Weapon, Meld into Stone, Revivify, Summon Fey, Dominate Beast, Greater Restoration, Summon Elemental.

### Spellcasting Focus (Level 2)
*New feature.*
Can use a druidic focus as a spellcasting focus for Ranger spells.

### Additional Fighting Styles
Ranger gains access to Blind Fighting, Druidic Warrior (see below), and Thrown Weapon Fighting.

**Druidic Warrior**: Learn two Druid cantrips. They count as Ranger spells, WIS-based. Replace one at any ASI level. Key cantrips: Shillelagh (WIS-based melee), Magic Stone.

### Primal Awareness (Level 3)
*Replaces Primeval Awareness (PHB).*
You can cast each of these spells once without expending a spell slot, PB times total per long rest: Speak with Animals (3rd), Beast Sense (5th), Speak with Plants (9th), Locate Creature (13th), Commune with Nature (17th). You learn these spells at the listed Ranger levels. Far more useful than the original Primeval Awareness.

### Nature's Veil (Level 10)
*Replaces Hide in Plain Sight (PHB).*
As a bonus action, become invisible until the start of your next turn. PB uses per long rest. Much better than the original — no setup time, works as a bonus action, and is actual invisibility.

### Martial Versatility (Level 4+)
At any ASI level, replace one Fighting Style with another.

## Rogue

### Steady Aim (Level 3)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
As a bonus action, give yourself advantage on your next attack roll on the current turn. Condition: your speed becomes 0 for the rest of the turn (no movement). Can't use if you've already moved this turn.

This is the ranged Rogue's best friend — guaranteed Sneak Attack every turn when you don't need to move. Competes with Cunning Action for the bonus action.

---

## Sorcerer

### Additional Sorcerer Spells
Significantly expands the Sorcerer's known spells list. Adds: Grease, Flame Blade, Flaming Sphere, Magic Weapon, Vampiric Touch, Fire Shield, Bigby's Hand, Flesh to Stone, Otiluke's Freezing Sphere, Demiplane.

### Sorcerous Versatility (Level 4+)
At any ASI level, swap one cantrip for another Sorcerer cantrip, OR swap one Metamagic option for another.

### Magical Guidance (Level 5)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
When you fail an ability check, you can spend 1 sorcery point to reroll. Must use the new roll. No action required — triggers on the failed check. A great use of leftover sorcery points.

---

## Warlock

### Additional Warlock Spells
Adds to the Warlock spell list: Mislead, Planar Binding, Teleportation Circle, Gate, Weird, plus various others depending on patron.

### Pact of the Talisman (Level 3)
*New Pact Boon option.*
Your patron gives you a talisman. When the wearer fails an ability check, they add 1d4 to the roll, potentially turning a failure into a success. PB uses per long rest. Can give the talisman to another creature. The talisman itself isn't destroyed if you die — you can make a new one during a short rest if lost.

### Eldritch Versatility (Level 4+)
At any ASI level, replace one cantrip with another Warlock cantrip, replace one Pact Boon with another, or replace one Eldritch Invocation with another (if you meet prereqs).

### New Eldritch Invocations

**Bond of the Talisman** (Prereq: Level 12, Pact of the Talisman): When the talisman wearer is on a different plane from you, either of you can use an action to teleport to the other's space. PB uses per long rest.

**Eldritch Mind** (No prereq): Advantage on Constitution saving throws to maintain concentration on a spell. Simple but strong — replaces the need for War Caster in many builds.

**Far Scribe** (Prereq: Level 5, Pact of the Tome): In your Book of Shadows, you can write the names of willing creatures (up to PB names). You can cast Sending targeting any named creature without using a spell slot or material components. Once per long rest per creature. To add a name, the creature must touch the Book.

**Gift of the Protectors** (Prereq: Level 9, Pact of the Tome): Up to PB willing creatures can write their names in your Book of Shadows. When a named creature drops to 0 HP, they instead drop to 1 HP. Each creature's name can trigger this once per long rest. A name vanishes from the book after triggering.

**Investment of the Chain Master** (Prereq: Pact of the Chain): Your familiar's attacks count as magical. When you take the Attack action, one attack can be replaced by the familiar's attack. The familiar's spell save DC becomes your Warlock spell save DC. Also, when the familiar takes damage, you can use your reaction to grant it resistance to that damage. Makes Pact of the Chain actually competitive.

**Protection of the Talisman** (Prereq: Level 7, Pact of the Talisman): When the talisman wearer fails a saving throw, they add 1d4, potentially succeeding. PB uses per long rest. Stacks conceptually with the base Talisman ability check bonus.

**Rebuke of the Talisman** (Prereq: Pact of the Talisman): When the talisman wearer takes damage from a creature, the wearer can use a reaction to deal psychic damage to the attacker equal to PB and push it 10 ft away. PB uses per long rest.

**Undying Servitude** (Prereq: Level 5): Cast Animate Dead once without a spell slot, once per long rest. Doesn't use a Pact Magic slot.

## Wizard

### Additional Wizard Spells
Adds to the Wizard spell list: Augury, Divination, Speak with Dead, and several TCoE summon spells.

### Cantrip Formulas (Level 3)
*New feature, replaces nothing.*
During a long rest, you can replace one Wizard cantrip you know with another from the Wizard spell list. This effectively makes Wizard cantrips prepared rather than permanently known — good flexibility.

Available to Fighter, Paladin, and/or Ranger as noted:

## New Battle Master Maneuvers

**Ambush**: Add superiority die to a Stealth check or initiative roll.

**Bait and Switch**: When you're within 5 ft of an ally, use 5 ft of movement to swap places (no OA). One of you (your choice) adds the superiority die to AC until start of your next turn.

**Brace**: When a creature you can see enters your reach, use your reaction to make one weapon attack. On hit, add superiority die to damage.

**Commanding Presence**: Add superiority die to a Charisma (Intimidation, Performance, or Persuasion) check.

**Grappling Strike**: After hitting with a melee attack, add superiority die to damage and attempt to grapple the target. Add the die to your Athletics check for the grapple.

**Quick Toss**: As a bonus action, draw a thrown weapon and make a ranged attack with it. Add superiority die to the damage. Costs one superiority die.

**Tactical Assessment**: Add superiority die to an Investigation, History, or Insight check.

## New Metamagic Options

**Seeking Spell** (2 sorcery points): If you miss with a spell attack roll, reroll the d20 and use the new roll. Can change a miss into a hit. Only works on attack roll spells.

**Transmuted Spell** (1 sorcery point): Change the damage type of a spell to acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder. The original damage type must be one of those six. Doesn't work on spells dealing non-elemental damage (radiant, necrotic, force, psychic, etc.).
