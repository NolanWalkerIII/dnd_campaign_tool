# Expansion Spells Reference

New spells from Xanathar's Guide to Everything (XGtE) and Tasha's Cauldron of Everything (TCoE). Organized by level, alphabetical within each level. Each entry follows the same format as the core spells reference.

For spellcasting rules, see `06_SPELLCASTING.md`.

---

## Cantrips (Level 0)

**Booming Blade** | Evocation | 1 action | Self (5-ft radius) | S, M (melee weapon worth at least 1 sp) | 1 round
Make a melee weapon attack as part of casting. On hit, normal weapon damage. Target is sheathed in booming energy — if it willingly moves before the start of your next turn, it takes 1d8 thunder damage. Both the initial and movement damage scale: 1d8/2d8 at 5th, 2d8/3d8 at 11th, 3d8/4d8 at 17th. The movement damage only triggers on willing movement (not forced). Key cantrip for melee casters and Rogues.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE revision of XGtE)*

**Green-Flame Blade** | Evocation | 1 action | Self (5-ft radius) | S, M (melee weapon worth at least 1 sp) | Instant
Make a melee weapon attack as part of casting. On hit, normal weapon damage. Green fire leaps to a different creature within 5 ft of the target — the second creature takes fire damage equal to your spellcasting ability modifier. Scaling: at 5th level, primary target takes extra 1d8 fire, secondary takes 1d8 + mod. At 11th: 2d8/2d8+mod. At 17th: 3d8/3d8+mod.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE revision of XGtE)*

**Lightning Lure** | Evocation | 1 action | Self (15-ft radius) | V | Instant
Target within 15 ft makes STR save or is pulled up to 10 ft toward you. If it ends within 5 ft of you, it takes 1d8 lightning damage. Scales at 5/11/17. Good for pulling enemies into hazards or melee range.
*Classes: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE revision of XGtE)*

**Mind Sliver** | Enchantment | 1 action | 60 ft | V | 1 round
INT save or 1d6 psychic damage + subtract 1d4 from target's next saving throw before end of your next turn. Scales at 5/11/17. The save debuff is the real value — set up a Fireball or Hold Person on the next turn.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Sword Burst** | Conjuration | 1 action | Self (5-ft radius) | V | Instant
Each creature within 5 ft of you makes DEX save or takes 1d6 force damage. Scales at 5/11/17. AoE cantrip — situational but useful when surrounded.
*Classes: Artificer, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE revision of XGtE)*

## 1st Level

**Absorb Elements** | Abjuration | 1 reaction (when you take acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder damage) | Self | S | 1 round
Gain resistance to the triggering damage type until start of your next turn. Also, your next melee attack on your next turn deals extra 1d6 damage of the triggering type. Upcast: +1d6 per level above 1st. One of the best defensive reaction spells in the game — competes for reaction with Shield.
*Classes: Artificer, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Catapult** | Transmutation | 1 action | 60 ft | S | Instant
Choose one object weighing 1-5 lbs that isn't being worn or carried. It flies 90 ft in a direction you choose. If it hits a creature, the creature makes DEX save or takes 3d8 bludgeoning. Object stops on hit or at end of range. Upcast: weight limit +5 lbs and +1d8 per level above 1st.
*Classes: Artificer, Sorcerer, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Cause Fear** | Necromancy | 1 action | 60 ft | V | Concentration, 1 min
One creature (non-undead, non-construct) you can see makes WIS save or is frightened. Target repeats save at end of each turn, ending on success. Upcast: one additional target per level above 1st (each must be within 30 ft of another target).
*Classes: Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Ceremony** | Abjuration (ritual) | 1 hour | Touch | V, S, M (25 gp of powdered silver, consumed) | Instant
Perform a religious ceremony. Options: Atonement (restore alignment), Bless Water (create holy water), Coming of Age (+1d4 to one ability check within 24 hrs, once), Dedication (AC +2 at start of first combat in next 24 hrs, once), Funeral Rite (body can't be undead for 7 days), Wedding (both creatures get +2 AC while within 30 ft of each other for 7 days — only works once per creature ever). Each creature can only benefit from each ceremony once.
*Classes: Cleric, Paladin* *(XGtE)*

**Chaos Bolt** | Evocation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | Instant
Ranged spell attack. 2d8 + 1d6 damage. The two d8s determine damage type: 1=acid, 2=cold, 3=fire, 4=force, 5=lightning, 6=poison, 7=psychic, 8=thunder. If both d8s match, the bolt leaps to another target within 30 ft — make a new attack roll and roll damage again (can chain infinitely if doubles keep rolling). Upcast: +1d6 per level above 1st.
*Classes: Sorcerer* *(XGtE)*

**Ice Knife** | Conjuration | 1 action | 60 ft | S, M | Instant
Ranged spell attack, 1d10 piercing. Hit or miss, the shard explodes — each creature within 5 ft of the target makes DEX save or takes 2d6 cold damage. The explosion always happens. Upcast: +1d6 cold per level above 1st.
*Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Snare** | Abjuration | 1 minute | Touch | S, M (25 ft of rope, consumed) | 8 hours (until triggered)
Create a 5-ft radius trap. Creature entering the area makes DEX save (using your spell DC) or is hoisted 3 ft in the air and restrained. Creature can make INT save at end of each turn to figure out the trap and use an action to make Athletics or Arcana check vs your DC to free itself. Spot it with INT (Investigation or Arcana) vs. your spell save DC.
*Classes: Artificer, Druid, Ranger, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Tasha's Caustic Brew** | Evocation | 1 action | Self (30-ft line) | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 min
30-ft long, 5-ft wide line. Each creature in the line makes DEX save or is covered in acid. A covered creature takes 2d4 acid damage at the start of each of its turns. Creature or adjacent ally can use action to scrub off the acid (ending the effect). Upcast: +2d4 per level above 1st.
*Classes: Artificer, Sorcerer, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Zephyr Strike** | Transmutation | 1 bonus action | Self | V | Concentration, 1 min
Your movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks for the duration. Once during the spell, you gain advantage on one weapon attack roll. If that attack hits, deal extra 1d8 force damage. Hit or miss, your speed increases by 30 ft until end of that turn. Ranger-specific mobility spell.
*Classes: Ranger* *(XGtE)*

## 2nd Level

**Dragon's Breath** | Transmutation | 1 bonus action | Touch | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 min
Touch a willing creature. Choose acid, cold, fire, lightning, or poison. The creature can use an action to exhale a 15-ft cone. Each creature in the cone makes DEX save or takes 3d6 of the chosen type (half on save). Can exhale each turn for the duration. Upcast: +1d6 per level above 2nd. Works on familiars — they get the action to breathe.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Healing Spirit** | Conjuration | 1 bonus action | 60 ft | V, S | Concentration, 1 min
Create a 5-ft cube of healing spirit. When a creature you can see enters the spirit's space or starts its turn there, you can use your reaction to heal it for 1d6. The spirit can heal a total number of times equal to 1 + your spellcasting ability modifier (minimum twice). You can move the spirit 30 ft as a bonus action on your turns. Upcast: +1d6 per level above 2nd.
*Classes: Druid, Ranger* *(XGtE)*

**Mind Spike** | Divination | 1 action | 60 ft | S | Concentration, 1 hour
WIS save. 3d8 psychic on fail, half on success. For the duration, you know the target's location (can't be hidden or invisible to you) as long as it's on the same plane. If you concentrate on this spell, you don't lose concentration by casting another Divination spell — the Mind Spike just ends. Upcast: +1d8 per level above 2nd.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Shadow Blade** | Illusion | 1 bonus action | Self | V, S | Concentration, 1 min
Create a sword of solidified shadow. It's a simple melee weapon, light, finesse, thrown (20/60). Deals 2d8 psychic damage. You have advantage on attack rolls with it against targets in dim light or darkness. If you drop it or throw it, it dissipates and reforms in your hand. Upcast: 3d8 at 3rd-4th, 4d8 at 5th-6th, 5d8 at 7th+.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Summon Beast** | Conjuration | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M (feather, tuft of fur, fish tail — 200 gp) | Concentration, 1 hour
Summon a bestial spirit. Choose Air, Land, or Water form. Uses a standardized stat block that scales with spell level. The spirit acts on your initiative and obeys your commands. HP = 20 + 5 per spell level above 2nd. AC = 11 + spell level. Multiattack at 2 attacks. Attack bonus = your spell attack bonus. Damage = 1d8 + 4 + spell level. Air: flyby (no OA). Land: pack tactics (advantage if ally within 5 ft). Water: swim speed.
*Classes: Druid, Ranger* *(TCoE)*

**Tasha's Mind Whip** | Enchantment | 1 action | 90 ft | V | 1 round
INT save or 3d6 psychic damage + target can use only one of the following on its next turn: action, bonus action, or movement (not reaction). On success: half damage, no other effect. Upcast: one additional target per level above 2nd (within 30 ft of each other).
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Warding Wind** | Evocation | 1 action | Self | V | Concentration, 10 min
Strong wind in 10-ft radius around you. Deafens you and other creatures in the area. Extinguishes unprotected flames. Area is difficult terrain for creatures other than you. Ranged weapon attacks passing through the wind have disadvantage. Disperses vapor, gas, and fog.
*Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

## 3rd Level

**Enemies Abound** | Enchantment | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | Concentration, 1 min
INT save. On fail, the target can't distinguish friend from foe. It treats all creatures as enemies. When it needs to choose a target, it randomly determines from available creatures in range. Makes opportunity attacks against anything moving away. Creature repeats save when it takes damage. Doesn't affect creatures immune to being frightened.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Life Transference** | Necromancy | 1 action | 30 ft | V, S | Instant
You take 4d8 necrotic damage (can't be reduced). One creature within range regains HP equal to twice the necrotic damage you took. So you sacrifice ~18 HP to heal ~36 HP. Upcast: +1d8 per level above 3rd.
*Classes: Cleric, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Spirit Shroud** | Necromancy | 1 bonus action | Self | V, S | Concentration, 1 min
Spirits surround you in a 10-ft radius. When you hit a creature within 10 ft with an attack, deal extra 1d8 of your choice: radiant, necrotic, or cold (chosen when cast). Creatures within 10 ft can't regain HP. Upcast: +1d8 at 5th, 7th, 9th. Stacks with Extra Attack — damage applies per hit. Strong competitor to Hex/Hunter's Mark for melee builds.
*Classes: Cleric, Paladin, Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Summon Fey** | Conjuration | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M (gilded flower, 300 gp) | Concentration, 1 hour
Summon a fey spirit. Choose Fuming (bonus action teleport 30 ft + advantage on next attack), Mirthful (charm on hit, WIS save), or Tricksy (can turn invisible as bonus action, auto-reveals on attack/spell/force save). HP = 30 + 10 per level above 3rd. AC = 12 + spell level. 2 attacks. Attack = your spell attack. Damage = 1d6 + 3 + spell level.
*Classes: Druid, Ranger, Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Summon Shadowspawn** | Conjuration | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M (tears inside a gem, 300 gp) | Concentration, 1 hour
Choose Fury (bonus action frighten), Despair (reduce speed to 0 on hit, WIS save), or Fear (frighten within 5 ft, WIS save). HP = 35 + 15 per level above 3rd. AC = 11 + spell level. Multiattack. In dim light/darkness: advantage on attacks. Attack = your spell attack. Damage = 1d12 + 3 + spell level.
*Classes: Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Summon Undead** | Necromancy | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M (gilded skull, 300 gp) | Concentration, 1 hour
Choose Ghostly (can phase through walls, fly 40 ft), Putrid (poison aura — CON save or poisoned), or Skeletal (ranged attack, 150 ft, bonus action extra shot). HP = 30 + 10 per level above 3rd. AC = 11 + spell level. Attack = your spell attack. Damage varies by form (1d8/1d12 + 3 + spell level).
*Classes: Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Thunder Step** | Conjuration | 1 action | 90 ft | V | Instant
Teleport to a space you can see within 90 ft. Thunder damage to everything within 10 ft of where you left — 3d10 thunder, CON save for half. You can bring one willing creature within 5 ft of your starting position — it appears within 5 ft of where you arrive. Upcast: +1d10 per level above 3rd.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Tiny Servant** | Transmutation | 1 minute | Touch | V, S | 8 hours
Animate a Tiny nonmagical object. It becomes a construct servant (AC 15, HP 10, STR 4, DEX 16, speed 30/climb 30). Can carry up to 10 lbs. Obeys your commands. Can attack: +5 to hit, 1d4+3 force damage. Upcast: animate 2 additional objects per level above 3rd.
*Classes: Artificer, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

## 4th Level

**Charm Monster** | Enchantment | 1 action | 30 ft | V, S | 1 hour
WIS save (advantage if you/allies are fighting it). On fail, charmed for the duration. Knows it was charmed when the spell ends. Basically Charm Person but works on any creature type. Upcast: one additional creature per level above 4th (within 30 ft of each other).
*Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Find Greater Steed** | Conjuration | 10 minutes | 30 ft | V, S | Instant (permanent until dismissed/killed)
Summon a greater steed: griffon, pegasus, peryton, dire wolf, rhinoceros, or saber-toothed tiger (or similar CR creature, DM's discretion). Has the creature's stat block but its type becomes celestial, fey, or fiend (your choice). Intelligence minimum 6. Communicates telepathically within 1 mile. Can share spells targeting Self with the steed.
*Classes: Paladin* *(XGtE)*

**Shadow of Moil** | Necromancy | 1 action | Self | V, S, M (undead eyeball in a gem, 150 gp) | Concentration, 1 min
Flame-like shadows surround you. You are heavily obscured to others (effectively invisible to normal vision). You have resistance to radiant damage. When a creature within 10 ft hits you with an attack, it takes 2d8 necrotic damage. Dim light within 10 ft of you becomes darkness. Strong self-buff, competes with Greater Invisibility for concentration.
*Classes: Warlock* *(XGtE)*

**Sickening Radiance** | Evocation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | Concentration, 10 min
30-ft radius sphere of sickly green light (dim). Creatures entering or starting turn in the area make CON save or take 4d10 radiant damage and gain one level of exhaustion (no save prevents the exhaustion on a failed save). Exhaustion stacks — 6 levels = death. Area is difficult terrain and heavily obscured. Combine with Wall of Force for "the microwave" combo.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Storm Sphere** | Evocation | 1 action | 150 ft | V, S | Concentration, 1 min
20-ft radius sphere. Each creature in the sphere when it appears or starts its turn there makes STR save or takes 2d6 bludgeoning. Area is difficult terrain. As a bonus action each turn, you can hurl a bolt of lightning from the sphere at a creature within 60 ft of it — ranged spell attack, 4d6 lightning. Upcast: +1d6 to both damage types per level above 4th.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Summon Aberration** | Conjuration | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M (pickled tentacle, 400 gp) | Concentration, 1 hour
Choose Beholderkin (ranged eye ray), Slaad (regenerate 5 HP/turn), or Star Spawn (psychic aura — 2d6 psychic to those within 5 ft at start of its turn). HP = 40 + 10 per level above 4th. AC = 11 + spell level. 2 attacks. Attack = your spell attack.
*Classes: Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Summon Construct** | Conjuration | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M (ornate lockbox, 400 gp) | Concentration, 1 hour
Choose Clay (bludgeoning attacks + reaction to impose disadvantage on attacks against it), Metal (heated body — deals fire damage to melee attackers), or Stone (lethal topple on hit — CON save or knocked prone). HP = 40 + 15 per level above 4th. AC = 13 + spell level. 2 attacks. Attack = your spell attack.
*Classes: Artificer, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Summon Elemental** | Conjuration | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M (air/earth/fire/water in a gold vial, 400 gp) | Concentration, 1 hour
Choose Air (fly 40 ft, whirlwind knocks prone), Earth (burrow 40 ft, advantage on attacks if target is on ground), Fire (ignite flammable objects, fire damage to melee attackers), Water (swim 90 ft, acid damage on hit). HP = 50 + 10 per level above 4th. AC = 11 + spell level. 2 attacks. Attack = your spell attack.
*Classes: Druid, Ranger, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

## 5th Level

**Danse Macabre** | Necromancy | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S | Concentration, 1 hour
Animate up to 5 Small or Medium corpses within range as zombies or skeletons (your choice per corpse). They obey your commands. On each of your turns, use bonus action to mentally command them all. They add your spellcasting ability modifier to their attack and damage rolls. Upcast: +2 undead per level above 5th.
*Classes: Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Dawn** | Evocation | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S, M (sunburst pendant, 100 gp) | Concentration, 1 min
30-ft radius, 40-ft tall cylinder of bright sunlight. Each creature in the area when it appears or starts its turn there makes CON save or takes 4d10 radiant damage (half on save). Counts as sunlight for vampire/other creature vulnerabilities. You can move the cylinder 60 ft as a bonus action.
*Classes: Cleric, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Enervation** | Necromancy | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S | Concentration, 1 min
DEX save. On fail, 4d8 necrotic and you can use your action each subsequent turn to automatically deal 4d8 necrotic (no save, no attack roll). On success: half damage and spell ends. Each time you deal damage, you heal half the necrotic dealt. On failed save this is massive sustained damage — 4d8 per turn with self-healing. Upcast: +1d8 per level above 5th.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Holy Weapon** | Evocation | 1 bonus action | Touch | V, S | Concentration, 1 hour
A weapon you touch deals an extra 2d8 radiant damage on each hit for the duration. You can dismiss the spell early (no action) to create a burst — each creature within 30 ft of the weapon bearer makes CON save or takes 4d8 radiant and is blinded for 1 minute (half damage, no blind on save, repeat save at end of each turn). Stacks with Extra Attack.
*Classes: Cleric, Paladin* *(XGtE)*

**Steel Wind Strike** | Conjuration | 1 action | 30 ft | S, M (melee weapon worth 1+ sp) | Instant
Choose up to 5 creatures within range. Make a melee spell attack against each. On hit: 6d10 force damage. You then teleport to an unoccupied space within 5 ft of one of the targets (hit or miss). Each attack is a separate attack roll. 30d10 force damage potential across 5 targets. Stylish and strong.
*Classes: Ranger, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Summon Celestial** | Conjuration | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M (golden reliquary, 500 gp) | Concentration, 1 hour
Choose Avenger (fly 40 ft, radiant bonus damage, 2 attacks) or Defender (temporary HP to allies within 10 ft at end of its turn). HP = 40 + 10 per level above 5th. AC = 11 + spell level (14 for Defender). Heals 2d6 with Healing Touch. Attack = your spell attack.
*Classes: Cleric* *(TCoE)*

**Synaptic Static** | Enchantment | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | Instant
20-ft radius sphere. Each creature with INT 2+ makes INT save or takes 8d6 psychic (half on save). On fail, target also subtracts 1d6 from attack rolls, ability checks, and concentration saves for 1 minute (save at end of each turn to end this effect). Essentially Fireball (psychic, INT save) with a debuff rider. One of the best 5th-level spells in the game.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Wall of Light** | Evocation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 10 min
60×5×10 ft wall of light. Creatures within 10 ft of wall when it appears or entering its space: CON save or 4d8 radiant + blinded for 1 minute (half, no blind on save). As an action, shoot a beam from the wall: ranged spell attack, 4d8 radiant. Emits bright light 120 ft.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

## 6th Level

**Investiture of Flame** | Transmutation | 1 action | Self | V, S | Concentration, 10 min
Fire resistance, immunity to cold damage. Shed bright light 30 ft, dim 30 ft. Adjacent creatures take 1d10 fire when they start turn or enter within 5 ft. As an action, shoot a 15×5 ft fire line: DEX save, 4d8 fire (half on save).
*Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Investiture of Ice** | Transmutation | 1 action | Self | V, S | Concentration, 10 min
Cold resistance, immunity to fire damage. Walk across water (freezes under your feet). Ground within 10 ft is difficult terrain for others. As an action: 15-ft cone, CON save, 4d6 cold (half on save). On fail: speed 0 for 1 round.
*Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Investiture of Stone** | Transmutation | 1 action | Self | V, S | Concentration, 10 min
Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, slashing from nonmagical attacks. Can move through earth/stone (if you end turn in it, you're shunted out and take 5d6). As an action, create a 15-ft radius tremor: DEX save or knocked prone.
*Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Investiture of Wind** | Transmutation | 1 action | Self | V, S | Concentration, 10 min
Ranged weapon attacks against you have disadvantage. Fly speed 60 ft. As an action: 15-ft cube wind blast within 60 ft. Each creature in it makes CON save or takes 2d10 bludgeoning and is pushed 10 ft (half, no push on save).
*Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Mental Prison** | Illusion | 1 action | 60 ft | S | Concentration, 1 min
One creature (INT 4+) makes INT save or takes 5d10 psychic and is trapped in an illusory prison. Target is restrained and sees the prison as whatever terrifies it most. If the target moves or is moved, or is targeted by a spell/effect originating outside, the prison shatters and deals 10d10 psychic. On initial save success: half damage, spell ends.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Scatter** | Conjuration | 1 action | 30 ft | V | Instant
Choose up to 5 creatures within range. Each makes CHA save (willing creatures auto-fail). Each target is teleported to an unoccupied space you can see within 120 ft of you. Great for repositioning allies or dispersing enemy groups.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Soul Cage** | Necromancy | 1 reaction (when a humanoid within 60 ft dies) | 60 ft | V, S, M (tiny silver cage, 100 gp) | 8 hours
Trap the soul. While you have it, you can use it (6 uses total before soul dissipates): Steal Life (bonus action, regain 2d8 HP), Query Soul (ask it a question, it answers truthfully — limited to what it knew in life), Borrow Experience (bonus action, advantage on your next attack/check/save), Eyes of the Dead (bonus action, see through the dead creature's eyes for 10 min, darkvision 120 ft in its location).
*Classes: Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Summon Fiend** | Conjuration | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M (humanoid blood in a ruby vial, 600 gp) | Concentration, 1 hour
Choose Demon (destruction aura), Devil (wall of fire), or Yugoloth (teleport 60 ft). HP = 50 + 15 per level above 6th. AC = 12 + spell level. Multiattack. Attack = your spell attack.
*Classes: Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Tasha's Otherworldly Guise** | Transmutation | 1 bonus action | Self | V, S, M (object engraved with planar symbol, 500 gp) | Concentration, 1 min
Choose Lower Planes or Upper Planes. You gain: fire and poison immunity + condition immunities (Lower) OR radiant and necrotic immunity + condition immunities (Upper). Plus: AC = spell ability mod + DEX if not wearing armor. Fly 40 ft. +2d8 damage on weapon attacks of the appropriate type. Two weapon attacks per Attack action. Immunity to specific conditions.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Tenser's Transformation** | Transmutation | 1 action | Self | V, S, M (a few hairs from a bull) | Concentration, 10 min
50 temporary HP. Advantage on attack rolls with simple and martial weapons. Weapon attacks deal extra 2d12 force damage. Proficiency with all armor, shields, simple and martial weapons, STR and CON saves. You can attack twice per Attack action. Can't cast spells or concentrate on other spells. When spell ends: one level of exhaustion.
*Classes: Wizard* *(XGtE)*

## 7th Level

**Crown of Stars** | Evocation | 1 action | Self | V, S | 1 hour (no concentration)
Seven star-like motes orbit your head. Shed dim light 30 ft. As a bonus action, hurl a mote: ranged spell attack, 4d12 radiant. While you have 4+ motes, shed bright light 30 ft. Spell ends when all motes expended. No concentration — this is the key selling point. 7 bonus action attacks at 4d12 each. Upcast: +2 motes per level above 7th.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Dream of the Blue Veil** | Conjuration | 10 minutes | 20 ft | V, S, M (magic item or willing creature from destination) | 6 hours
You and up to 8 willing creatures fall unconscious and travel to another world/plane of existence. Wake up there after 6 hours (or when spell ends). Primarily a campaign/narrative spell — the DM determines which world you reach based on the focus item.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(TCoE)*

**Power Word Pain** | Enchantment | 1 action | 60 ft | V | Instant
Target a creature with 100 HP or fewer. It's wracked with pain — speed max 10 ft, disadvantage on attack rolls, ability checks, and saves (except CON), requires CON save to cast any spell. Target can make CON save at end of each turn to end. No save to apply — if it's at ≤100 HP, it just works.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Temple of the Gods** | Conjuration | 1 hour | 120 ft | V, S, M (holy symbol, 5 gp) | 24 hours
Create a 120×120 ft temple. Lasts 24 hours (casting every day for a year makes it permanent). The interior repels specific creature types (celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, or undead — choose up to all of them). Barred creatures can't enter, can't charm/frighten/possess those inside. Interior has comfortable furnishing and enough food for 100 Medium creatures.
*Classes: Cleric* *(XGtE)*

## 8th Level

**Abi-Dalzim's Horrid Wilting** | Necromancy | 1 action | 150 ft | V, S, M | Instant
30-ft radius sphere. Each creature makes CON save — 12d8 necrotic on fail, half on save. Plant creatures and magical plants have disadvantage. Nonmagical plants in the area wither and die. Water elementals and creatures made of water have disadvantage. Does not affect undead or constructs.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard* *(XGtE)*

**Illusory Dragon** | Illusion | 1 action | 120 ft | S | Concentration, 1 min
Create a Huge illusory dragon in an unoccupied space. When it appears, each creature within 60 ft that can see it makes WIS save or is frightened for 1 minute. As a bonus action, move the dragon 60 ft. When it moves through a creature's space or a creature enters its space: that creature makes INT save or takes 7d6 of a damage type you chose when you cast the spell (acid, cold, fire, lightning, necrotic, or poison). Half on save. Can also use it as a 60-ft breath weapon cone once per turn.

**Maddening Darkness** | Evocation | 1 action | 150 ft | V, M (drop of pitch with a stick of charcoal) | Concentration, 10 min

## TCoE Summon Spell Design Notes

TCoE introduced a new standardized summoning system. Key points for LLM reference:

- Each summon spell has a **fixed stat block** that scales with spell level — no need to look up individual monster stats
- The summoned creature acts on **your initiative count** (not a separate roll)
- Commands require **no action** (it obeys verbal commands; if no command, it Dodges)
- Material components are **not consumed** but have a gold cost (requires having the item)
- All summons last **1 hour** with concentration
- Attack bonus and save DC use **your spell attack modifier and spell save DC**
- HP, AC, and damage scale predictably with spell level upcast

This design replaced the older "summon 8 wolves and crash the game" model from PHB. Much cleaner for gameplay and LLM tracking.
