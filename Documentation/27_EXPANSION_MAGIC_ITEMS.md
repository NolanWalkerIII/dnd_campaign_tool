# Expansion Magic Items Reference

Magic items from Xanathar's Guide to Everything (XGtE) and Tasha's Cauldron of Everything (TCoE). This supplements the core Magic Items Reference (`12_MAGIC_ITEMS.md`).

---

## Common Magic Items (XGtE)

XGtE introduced Common rarity magic items — low-power items meant for flavor and utility rather than combat power. No attunement unless noted. Great for early levels and worldbuilding.

### Weapons & Armor

**Moon-Touched Sword** (Common): Any sword. In darkness, blade sheds bright light 15 ft, dim 15 ft. The weapon counts as magical for overcoming resistance. The cheapest way to get a magic weapon.

**Cast-Off Armor** (Common): Any armor. You can doff this armor as an action (instead of the normal time). Useful for druids or shapeshifters who need to remove armor quickly.

**Smoldering Armor** (Common): Any armor. Wisps of harmless, odorless smoke rise from the armor. Purely cosmetic.

### Utility & Flavor

**Cloak of Many Fashions** (Common): As a bonus action, change the style, color, and apparent quality of the cloak. It can't replicate a specific individual's cloak.

**Clockwork Amulet** (Common): Once per day, when you make an attack roll, you can forgo rolling and instead take a 10 on the d20. Decide before rolling. No attunement. Guaranteed minimum attack roll.

**Dread Helm** (Common): Helmet whose visor makes your eyes glow red while worn. Purely cosmetic.

**Enduring Spellbook** (Common): This spellbook and anything written in it can't be damaged by fire or immersion in water. Solves a real Wizard worry.

**Hat of Vermin** (Common): 3 charges, regains 1d3 at dawn. As an action, pull a bat, frog, or rat from the hat. The creature is ordinary and vanishes after 1 hour or at 0 HP.

**Hat of Wizardry** (Common, attunement by Wizard): This hat lets you use it as a spellcasting focus. Once per long rest, you can try to cast a Wizard cantrip you don't know — DC 10 Arcana check or the spell fails.

**Heward's Handy Spice Pouch** (Common): 10 charges, regains 1d6+4 at dawn. As an action, produce a pinch of any spice. Critical for the Chef feat.

**Instrument of Illusions** (Common, attunement by Bard): While playing, you can create harmless illusory visual effects within 5 ft. Effects end when you stop playing.

**Mystery Key** (Common): Chance to unlock one lock it touches, then turns to copper. DM determines if the lock yields.

**Orb of Direction** (Common): As an action, determine which way is magnetic north. Only works on the Material Plane.

**Perfume of Bewitching** (Common): Apply as an action. For 1 hour, advantage on CHA (Persuasion) checks against humanoids within 5 ft. Single use.

**Pipe of Smoke Monsters** (Common): As an action, exhale a puff of smoke that takes the shape of a single creature (your choice). The smoke monster is recognizable but obviously made of smoke.

**Pole of Angling** (Common): 10-ft pole that functions as a fishing rod. Can fish perfectly.

**Pot of Awakening** (Common): Plant an ordinary shrub. After 30 days, the shrub becomes an Awakened Shrub (CR 0) friendly to you. The pot becomes nonmagical.

**Ruby of the War Mage** (Common, attunement by spellcaster): Attach to a weapon over a short rest. While attached, you can use that weapon as a spellcasting focus. Solves "hands full" issues for gish characters.

**Staff of Adornment** (Common): Tiny floating objects orbit the staff's tip. Can place objects up to 1 lb each, up to 3 at a time.

**Staff of Birdcalls** (Common): 10 charges, regains 1d6+4 at dawn. As an action, expend 1 charge to create bird sounds.

**Staff of Flowers** (Common): 10 charges, regains 1d6+4 at dawn. As an action, cause a flower to sprout from soil or the staff.

**Talking Doll** (Common, attunement): During a short rest, tell the doll a message of 25 words or fewer. It repeats the message when a creature touches it or comes within 5 ft (your choice of trigger). Repeats until the message is given again.

**Tankard of Sobriety** (Common): You can drink any amount of alcohol from this tankard without becoming intoxicated.

**Unbreakable Arrow** (Common): Ammunition that can't be broken, except in an Antimagic Field. Recoverable after combat guaranteed.

**Walloping Ammunition** (Common): On hit, target makes DC 10 STR save or is knocked prone. Works with any ammunition.

## Magical Tattoos (TCoE)

TCoE introduced magic tattoos — applied by a tattoo needle (special tool). Once applied, the tattoo can't be moved. It occupies one of the body's tattoo slots. A creature can have one magic tattoo per body area.

### Tattoo Rules
- Applied with a special needle over a short rest
- Count against attunement slots
- A tattoo can be removed by the tattooist over a short rest, or by pressing a particular needle against it
- Tattoos can cover any reasonable body part

### Tattoo List

**Absorbing Tattoo** (Very Rare, attunement)
- Associated with a damage type (acid, cold, fire, force, lightning, necrotic, poison, psychic, radiant, or thunder)
- Resistance to the associated damage type
- Reaction: when you take damage of the associated type, gain immunity to it instead and regain HP equal to half the damage. Single use, then the tattoo vanishes.

**Barrier Tattoo** (Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare, attunement)
- Provides AC when not wearing armor:
  - Uncommon: AC 12 + DEX modifier
  - Rare: AC 15 + DEX modifier (max 2)
  - Very Rare: AC 18
- Replaces Unarmored Defense for monks that want higher base AC. The Very Rare version is essentially plate armor as a tattoo.

**Blood Fury Tattoo** (Legendary, attunement)
- 10 charges, regains 1d6+4 at dawn
- When you hit with a weapon or unarmed strike, spend 1 charge to deal extra 4d6 necrotic and regain HP equal to the necrotic damage dealt
- Reaction when damaged by a creature within 5 ft: spend 1 charge, make a melee attack against them

**Coiling Grasp Tattoo** (Uncommon, attunement)
- As an action, grapple a creature within 15 ft. Target makes DC 14 STR save or is grappled (escape DC 14). While grappled, takes 1d6 force damage at start of each turn. Twice per dawn.

**Eldritch Claw Tattoo** (Uncommon, attunement)
- Unarmed strikes count as magical. +1 to attack and damage with unarmed strikes.
- Once per dawn: bonus action to activate for 1 minute. While active: unarmed strike reach +15 ft, unarmed strikes deal extra 1d6 force damage.

**Ghost Step Tattoo** (Very Rare, attunement)
- 3 charges, regains 1 charge at dawn
- As a bonus action, spend 1 charge to become incorporeal until end of your next turn. While incorporeal: move through creatures and objects (take 1d10 force if you end turn inside), speed +10 ft, resistance to all damage except force.

**Illuminator's Tattoo** (Common, attunement)
- Shed bright light 5 ft as a bonus action (toggle)
- When you write, the tattoo glows — you can write in any language you know, even on surfaces like stone or metal, and the writing is magical and legible
- Produce a written message of 6 words or fewer that glows faintly for up to 7 days. Can produce 1 message per long rest.

**Lifewell Tattoo** (Very Rare, attunement)
- Resistance to necrotic damage
- Once per dawn: when reduced to 0 HP, drop to 1 HP instead

**Masquerade Tattoo** (Common, attunement)
- As a bonus action, change the tattoo's appearance to any art you want
- Once per dawn: cast Disguise Self (no components)

**Shadowfell Brand Tattoo** (Rare, attunement)
- Advantage on Stealth checks
- Darkvision 60 ft (or +30 ft if you already have darkvision)
- Reaction when you take damage: become insubstantial for a moment — resistance to all damage for that hit. Twice per dawn.

**Spellwrought Tattoo** (Varies, no attunement)
- Contains a single spell. As an action, activate the tattoo to cast the spell (using its normal casting time). The tattoo vanishes after use.
- Rarity based on spell level: Cantrip (Common), 1st (Common), 2nd (Uncommon), 3rd (Uncommon), 4th (Rare), 5th (Rare)
- Good for single-use spell access for non-casters

## Notable Uncommon-Rare Items (Expansion)

**All-Purpose Tool** (Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare, attunement by Artificer, TCoE)
- +1/+2/+3 to spell attack rolls and spell save DC
- As an action, transform into any artisan's tool. Gain proficiency with it while holding it.
- At start of day: choose one cantrip from any spell list. It counts as an Artificer cantrip for you until next long rest.
- *The Artificer's best friend. Essentially a wand of the war mage + free cantrip.*

**Amulet of the Devout** (Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare, attunement by Cleric or Paladin, TCoE)
- +1/+2/+3 to spell attack rolls and spell save DC
- Once per long rest: use Channel Divinity without expending a use

**Arcane Grimoire** (Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare, attunement by Wizard, TCoE)
- +1/+2/+3 to spell attack rolls and spell save DC
- Can use as spellcasting focus
- When you use Arcane Recovery, one spell slot recovered can be one level higher than normal

**Bloodwell Vial** (Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare, attunement by Sorcerer, TCoE)
- +1/+2/+3 to spell attack rolls and spell save DC
- Can use as spellcasting focus
- Once per dawn: when you roll Hit Dice during short rest, recover 5 sorcery points

**Moon Sickle** (Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare, attunement by Druid or Ranger, TCoE)
- +1/+2/+3 to attack/damage rolls AND spell attack rolls
- Can use as spellcasting focus
- When casting a spell that restores HP, the target regains 1d4 additional HP

**Rhythm-Maker's Drum** (Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare, attunement by Bard, TCoE)
- +1/+2/+3 to spell attack rolls and spell save DC
- Can use as spellcasting focus
- As an action, regain one use of Bardic Inspiration. Once per dawn.

**Rod of the Pact Keeper** (Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare, attunement by Warlock, DMG but worth noting)
- +1/+2/+3 to spell attack rolls and spell save DC
- Once per long rest: regain one Pact Magic spell slot

## TCoE Class-Specific Focus Items

TCoE established that every spellcasting class should have a dedicated magic focus item that scales with rarity. The pattern:

| Class | Item | Bonus Feature |
|---|---|---|
| Artificer | All-Purpose Tool | Free cantrip + tool transformation |
| Bard | Rhythm-Maker's Drum | Recover Bardic Inspiration |
| Cleric/Paladin | Amulet of the Devout | Extra Channel Divinity |
| Druid/Ranger | Moon Sickle | +1d4 healing on healing spells |
| Sorcerer | Bloodwell Vial | Recover sorcery points |
| Warlock | Rod of the Pact Keeper | Recover Pact slot |
| Wizard | Arcane Grimoire | Improved Arcane Recovery |

All provide +1/+2/+3 to spell attack rolls and spell save DC at Uncommon/Rare/Very Rare.

## Notable Artifacts & Legendary Items

**Mighty Servant of Leuk-o** (Artifact, TCoE)
- 10-ft tall construct mech suit. Two creatures can control it (pilot + gunner)
- AC 22, HP 500, Speed 60 ft
- Fist: +15, 4d12+10 bludgeoning. Crush: grappled creature takes 16d12+10 bludgeoning (DEX DC 20)
- Spell immunity (most), siege monster (double damage to structures)
- Random properties, sentient, can go berserk

**Crook of Rao** (Artifact, TCoE)
- Banishes fiends: all fiends within 1 mile make CHA DC 20 or banished for 100 years
- 6 charges: Aura of Life, Aura of Purity, Banishment, Beacon of Hope, Mass Cure Wounds
- Sentient (LG, telepathy 120 ft)
- After major banishment, 1d100 days later the banished fiends send agents after the wielder

**Demonomicon of Iggwilv** (Artifact, TCoE)
- While carrying: advantage on saves vs. spells and effects from fiends, DC 20 Investigation/Insight to detect falsehoods
- 8 charges: Magic Circle, Gate, Planar Binding, Summon Fiend
- Demonic Tutor: advantage on Arcana checks about demons. Can learn a demon's truename with 24 hours study.
- Sentient (CE), wants to summon demons
