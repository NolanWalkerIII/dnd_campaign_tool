## Encounter Difficulty Quick Calculator

For a party of 4 at a given level, here are rough monster counts for a medium encounter:

| Party Level | 1 Monster at CR | OR Multiple Monsters |
|-------------|----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | CR 1 | 4× CR 1/4 |
| 2 | CR 2 | 3× CR 1/2 |
| 3 | CR 3 | 4× CR 1/2 or 2× CR 1 |
| 4 | CR 4 | 2× CR 2 or 6× CR 1/2 |
| 5 | CR 5 | 2× CR 3 or 4× CR 1 |
| 6-7 | CR 6-7 | 3× CR 3 |
| 8-10 | CR 8-10 | 2× CR 5 or 4× CR 3 |
| 11-14 | CR 11-14 | Mix of CR 5-8 creatures |
| 15-20 | CR 15-20 | Boss + minions structure |

These are guidelines. Actual difficulty depends on party composition, magic items, terrain, and tactics.
