# Multiverse Races Reference

Races from Mordenkainen Presents: Monsters of the Multiverse (MotM) plus Custom Lineage from Tasha's Cauldron of Everything.

## MotM Changes

MotM revised nearly all non-PHB races. Key changes across the board:
- **Flexible ASIs**: All MotM races use +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1 to any ability scores (player's choice)
- **Creature types**: Many races are no longer Humanoid — Fey, Celestial, etc.
- **Innate spellcasting**: Uses spell slots if you have them, otherwise free casts. Casting ability is your choice of INT, WIS, or CHA (chosen at character creation)
- **Proficiency-scaling uses**: Features that used to be 1/long rest now often scale with PB uses per long rest
- These versions supersede Volo's Guide and Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes

---

## Custom Lineage (TCoE)

For building a race from scratch when nothing published fits.

- **ASI**: +2 to one ability score
- **Size**: Small or Medium (your choice)
- **Speed**: 30 ft walking
- **Feat**: One feat at level 1 (must meet prerequisites)
- **Darkvision OR Skill**: Choose either darkvision 60 ft or one skill proficiency
- **Languages**: Common + one other
- **Creature Type**: Humanoid

Mechanically similar to Variant Human but slightly more flexible with the darkvision option.

## MotM Races (Alphabetical)

### Aarakocra

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft walking, 30 ft flying
- **Traits**:
  - **Flight**: Fly speed equal to walking speed. Can't fly in medium or heavy armor.
  - **Talons**: Unarmed strikes deal 1d6 + STR slashing damage.
  - **Wind Caller**: Cast Gust of Wind without a spell slot once per long rest. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

Flight at level 1 is strong. Some DMs ban or restrict this.

### Aasimar

- **Type**: Celestial (not Humanoid)
- **Size**: Medium or Small (your choice)
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Celestial Resistance**: Resistance to necrotic and radiant damage.
  - **Healing Hands**: As an action, touch a creature and heal HP equal to PB. PB uses per long rest.
  - **Light Bearer**: Know the Light cantrip. Casting ability: CHA.
  - **Celestial Revelation** (Level 3): As a bonus action, choose one transformation once per long rest, lasts 1 minute:
    - **Necrotic Shroud**: Creatures within 10 ft make CHA save (DC 8 + CHA + PB) or frightened of you until end of your next turn. While transformed, once per turn deal extra necrotic damage equal to your PB on one attack/spell.
    - **Radiant Consumption**: Shed bright light 10 ft, dim 10 ft. At the end of each of your turns, you and each creature within 10 ft take radiant damage equal to PB. Once per turn, deal extra radiant damage equal to PB on one attack/spell.
    - **Radiant Soul**: Fly speed equal to walking speed (spectral wings). Once per turn, deal extra radiant damage equal to PB on one attack/spell.

Pick your transformation each time you use it — not locked in at character creation.

### Bugbear

- **Type**: Fey (not Humanoid)
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charmed.
  - **Long-Limbed**: Melee attacks have +5 ft reach on your turn only. Does not increase opportunity attack range.
  - **Powerful Build**: Count as one size larger for carrying capacity and push/drag/lift.
  - **Sneaky**: Proficiency in Stealth.
  - **Surprise Attack**: If you hit a creature that hasn't taken a turn in combat yet, deal an extra 2d6 damage. Once per combat.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Centaur

- **Type**: Fey
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 40 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Charge**: If you move at least 30 ft straight toward a target and hit with a melee weapon attack on the same turn, you can use a bonus action to make one attack with your hooves against the same target.
  - **Equine Build**: Count as one size larger for carrying capacity. Climbing costs 4× movement instead of 2×. Any climb that requires hands and feet is effectively impossible.
  - **Hooves**: Unarmed strikes deal 1d6 + STR bludgeoning damage.
  - **Natural Affinity**: Proficiency in one of: Animal Handling, Medicine, Nature, or Survival.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Changeling

- **Type**: Fey
- **Size**: Medium or Small (your choice)
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Shapechanger**: As an action, change your appearance (any humanoid form you've seen, same general size). Extends to clothing and equipment appearance but not function. A creature can see through it with Investigation vs. your Deception.
  - **Changeling Instincts**: Proficiency in two of: Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Persuasion.
- **Languages**: Common + two others

### Deep Gnome (Svirfneblin)

- **Type**: Humanoid (Gnome)
- **Size**: Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 120 ft
  - **Gnome Cunning**: Advantage on INT, WIS, and CHA saving throws against spells and magic.
  - **Gift of the Svirfneblin**: Cast Disguise Self once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Starting at 3rd level, cast Nondetection without a spell slot (targeting self only) once per long rest. At 5th level, cast Blindness/Deafness once per long rest without a spell slot. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
  - **Svirfneblin Camouflage**: Advantage on Stealth checks to hide in rocky terrain.
- **Languages**: Common, Gnomish, Undercommon

### Duergar

- **Type**: Humanoid (Dwarf)
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 120 ft
  - **Duergar Resilience**: Advantage on saves vs. poison, charmed, and stunned. Resistance to poison damage.
  - **Psionic Fortitude**: Advantage on saves vs. charmed and stunned.
  - **Duergar Magic**: Cast Enlarge/Reduce (enlarge only, self only) once per long rest without a spell slot, starting at 3rd level. Cast Invisibility (self only) once per long rest without a spell slot, starting at 5th level. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
  - **Sunlight Sensitivity**: Disadvantage on attack rolls and Perception checks that rely on sight when in direct sunlight.
- **Languages**: Common, Dwarvish

### Eladrin

- **Type**: Fey (Elf)
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charmed, immune to magical sleep.
  - **Trance**: 4-hour semiconscious rest instead of 8-hour sleep.
  - **Keen Senses**: Proficiency in Perception.
  - **Fey Step**: Bonus action teleport 30 ft to an unoccupied space you can see. PB uses per long rest. Each use has a rider effect based on your current season (you choose your season; can change at any long rest):
    - **Autumn**: Up to 2 creatures within 10 ft of where you land must make WIS save (DC 8 + PB + casting ability mod) or be charmed by you for 1 minute (or until you/allies damage them).
    - **Winter**: One creature within 5 ft of where you land must make WIS save or be frightened of you until end of your next turn.
    - **Spring**: You can touch one willing creature within 5 ft before teleporting. That creature teleports instead of you.
    - **Summer**: Each creature within 5 ft of where you land takes fire damage equal to PB.
- **Languages**: Common, Elvish

### Fairy

- **Type**: Fey
- **Size**: Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft walking, 30 ft flying
- **Traits**:
  - **Flight**: Fly speed equal to walking speed. Can't use in medium or heavy armor.
  - **Fairy Magic**: Know Druidcraft cantrip. At 3rd level, cast Faerie Fire once per long rest without a spell slot. At 5th level, cast Enlarge/Reduce once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

Small flyer with innate spellcasting. Can squeeze through spaces as Small.

### Firbolg

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Firbolg Magic**: Cast Detect Magic and Disguise Self each once per long rest without spell slots. When Disguise Self is used, you can appear up to 3 ft shorter (to blend with shorter races). Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
  - **Hidden Step**: As a bonus action, become invisible until the start of your next turn (or until you attack, deal damage, or force a save). PB uses per long rest.
  - **Powerful Build**: Count as one size larger for carrying capacity.
  - **Speech of Beast and Leaf**: Can communicate simple ideas to beasts and plants. Advantage on CHA checks to influence them.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Genasi — Air

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium or Small
- **Speed**: 35 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Unending Breath**: Can hold your breath indefinitely while not incapacitated.
  - **Lightning Resistance**: Resistance to lightning damage.
  - **Mingle with the Wind**: Know Shocking Grasp cantrip. At 3rd level, cast Feather Fall once per long rest without a spell slot. At 5th level, cast Levitate once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
- **Languages**: Common, Primordial

### Genasi — Earth

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium or Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Earth Walk**: Can move across difficult terrain made of earth or stone without extra movement cost.
  - **Merge with Stone**: Know Blade Ward cantrip. At 5th level, cast Pass Without Trace once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
- **Languages**: Common, Primordial

### Genasi — Fire

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium or Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Fire Resistance**: Resistance to fire damage.
  - **Reach to the Blaze**: Know Produce Flame cantrip. At 3rd level, cast Burning Hands once per long rest without a spell slot. At 5th level, cast Flame Blade once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
- **Languages**: Common, Primordial

### Genasi — Water

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium or Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft walking, 30 ft swimming
- **Traits**:
  - **Amphibious**: Breathe air and water.
  - **Acid Resistance**: Resistance to acid damage.
  - **Call to the Wave**: Know Acid Splash cantrip. At 3rd level, cast Create or Destroy Water once per long rest without a spell slot. At 5th level, cast Water Walk once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
- **Languages**: Common, Primordial

### Githyanki

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Astral Knowledge**: At the end of a long rest, gain proficiency with one skill and one weapon or tool of your choice until end of your next long rest. Flexible daily proficiency.
  - **Githyanki Psionics**: Know Mage Hand cantrip (hand is invisible). At 3rd level, cast Jump once per long rest without a spell slot. At 5th level, cast Misty Step once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
  - **Psychic Resilience**: Resistance to psychic damage.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Githzerai

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Githzerai Psionics**: Know Mage Hand cantrip (hand is invisible). At 3rd level, cast Shield once per long rest without a spell slot. At 5th level, cast Detect Thoughts once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
  - **Mental Discipline**: Advantage on saving throws vs. charmed and frightened.
  - **Psychic Resilience**: Resistance to psychic damage.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

Shield once per long rest is notable — strong defensive option on a non-caster.

### Goblin

- **Type**: Fey (not Humanoid)
- **Size**: Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charmed.
  - **Fury of the Small**: When you damage a creature that is of a size larger than yours, you deal extra damage equal to PB. PB uses per long rest.
  - **Nimble Escape**: Disengage or Hide as a bonus action. Unlimited uses. This is the standout trait — it's effectively Cunning Action.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Goliath

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Little Giant**: Powerful Build (count as Large for carrying). Proficiency in Athletics.
  - **Mountain Born**: Resistance to cold damage. Acclimated to high altitude (no altitude sickness).
  - **Stone's Endurance**: When you take damage, use your reaction to roll 1d12 + CON modifier and reduce the damage by that total. PB uses per long rest.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Harengon

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium or Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Hare-Trigger**: Add PB to initiative rolls.
  - **Leporine Senses**: Proficiency in Perception.
  - **Lucky Footwork**: When you fail a DEX saving throw, add 1d4 to the result, potentially turning it into a success. Can't use if prone or speed is 0. PB uses per long rest.
  - **Rabbit Hop**: As a bonus action, jump a number of feet equal to 5 × PB without provoking opportunity attacks. PB uses per long rest.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

Initiative bonus + DEX save boost makes this a strong pick for any class.

### Hobgoblin

- **Type**: Fey (not Humanoid)
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charmed.
  - **Fey Gift**: You can use the Help action as a bonus action. PB uses per long rest. Starting at 3rd level, when you Help, choose a rider: the helped creature also gains temporary HP equal to PB (Hospitality), +10 ft speed until start of your next turn (Passage), or advantage on their next saving throw before your next turn starts (Spite).
  - **Fortune from the Many**: When you miss an attack, fail an ability check, or fail a saving throw, you can add 1d6 if you can see an ally within 30 ft. PB uses per long rest.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Kenku

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium or Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Expert Duplication**: Advantage on ability checks to produce forgeries or duplicates of existing objects.
  - **Kenku Recall**: PB times per long rest, you can give yourself advantage on an ability check using a skill you're proficient in.
  - **Mimicry**: Can mimic sounds and voices heard. Listener makes Insight vs. your Deception to determine it's fake.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

MotM removed the speech restriction. Kenku can now talk normally.

### Kobold

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Draconic Cry**: As a bonus action, let out a cry. Until start of your next turn, you and allies have advantage on attack rolls against enemies within 10 ft of you. PB uses per long rest.
  - **Kobold Legacy**: Choose one at character creation:
    - **Craftiness**: Proficiency in one of: Arcana, Investigation, Medicine, Sleight of Hand, Survival.
    - **Defiance**: Advantage on saving throws against the frightened condition.
    - **Draconic Sorcery**: Know one cantrip from the Sorcerer list. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
- **Languages**: Common, Draconic

Draconic Cry is strong — party-wide advantage in a radius with no save.

### Lizardfolk

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft, 30 ft swimming
- **Traits**:
  - **Bite**: Unarmed strike with your bite deals 1d6 + STR slashing damage.
  - **Hold Breath**: Hold breath for up to 15 minutes.
  - **Hungry Jaws**: As a bonus action, make a bite attack. If it hits, you gain temporary HP equal to PB. PB uses per long rest.
  - **Natural Armor**: AC = 13 + DEX modifier when not wearing armor. Can still use a shield.
  - **Nature's Intuition**: Proficiency in two of: Animal Handling, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Stealth, Survival.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

Natural Armor 13 + DEX is notable — matches scale mail with no stealth disadvantage.

### Minotaur

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Horns**: Unarmed strikes with your horns deal 1d6 + STR piercing damage.
  - **Goring Rush**: After using the Dash action, you can make one melee attack with your horns as a bonus action.
  - **Hammering Horns**: After hitting a creature with a melee attack (weapon or horns), you can use a bonus action to attempt to shove it. The creature makes a STR save (DC 8 + STR + PB) or is pushed 10 ft away from you.
  - **Labyrinthine Recall**: Always know which direction is north. Advantage on saves and checks to avoid getting lost.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Orc

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Adrenaline Rush**: As a bonus action, Dash. You gain temporary HP equal to PB when you do. PB uses per long rest.
  - **Powerful Build**: Count as one size larger for carrying capacity.
  - **Relentless Endurance**: When reduced to 0 HP but not killed outright, drop to 1 HP instead. Once per long rest.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

Relentless Endurance alone makes this competitive. Previously exclusive to Half-Orc.

### Satyr

- **Type**: Fey (not Humanoid)
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 35 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Ram**: Unarmed strikes with your head deal 1d6 + STR bludgeoning damage.
  - **Magic Resistance**: Advantage on saving throws against spells. One of the strongest racial traits in the game.
  - **Mirthful Leaps**: Whenever you make a long or high jump, add 1d8 ft to the distance (no action cost).
  - **Reveler**: Proficiency in Performance and Persuasion.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

Fey creature type + Magic Resistance makes Satyr one of the strongest mechanical race choices.

### Sea Elf

- **Type**: Humanoid (Elf)
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft walking, 30 ft swimming
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charmed, immune to magical sleep.
  - **Trance**: 4-hour rest.
  - **Keen Senses**: Proficiency in Perception.
  - **Child of the Sea**: Breathe air and water.
  - **Friend of the Sea**: Can communicate simple ideas with beasts that have a swimming speed.
- **Languages**: Common, Elvish + one other

### Shadar-kai

- **Type**: Humanoid (Elf)
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Fey Ancestry**: Advantage on saves vs. charmed, immune to magical sleep.
  - **Trance**: 4-hour rest.
  - **Keen Senses**: Proficiency in Perception.
  - **Necrotic Resistance**: Resistance to necrotic damage.
  - **Blessing of the Raven Queen**: Bonus action to teleport 30 ft to an unoccupied space you can see. After teleporting, you gain resistance to all damage types until the start of your next turn. PB uses per long rest.
- **Languages**: Common, Elvish + one other

Resistance to all damage for a round after teleporting is extremely strong at higher levels.

### Shifter

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Bestial Instincts**: Proficiency in one of: Acrobatics, Athletics, Intimidation, Survival.
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Shifting**: As a bonus action, gain temporary HP equal to PB + level, shift lasts 1 minute. PB uses per long rest. Additional benefit based on subrace:
    - **Beasthide**: +1 AC while shifted.
    - **Longtooth**: Fangs are natural weapons (1d6 + STR piercing). Can use bonus action on subsequent turns to bite while shifted.
    - **Swiftstride**: +10 ft walking speed while shifted. When a creature ends its turn within 5 ft of you, you can use your reaction to move up to 10 ft without provoking opportunity attacks.
    - **Wildhunt**: Advantage on WIS checks while shifted. No creature within 30 ft can make attack rolls with advantage against you (unless you're incapacitated).
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Tabaxi

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium or Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft, 20 ft climbing
- **Traits**:
  - **Cat's Claws**: Unarmed strikes deal 1d6 + STR slashing. Climb speed 20 ft.
  - **Cat's Talent**: Proficiency in Perception and Stealth.
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Feline Agility**: When you move on your turn, you can double your speed until end of that turn. Can't use again until you spend a turn with 0 movement (stand still for one turn to reset).
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Tortle

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium or Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Claws**: Unarmed strikes deal 1d6 + STR slashing.
  - **Hold Breath**: Up to 1 hour.
  - **Natural Armor**: AC = 17. Flat value — not modified by DEX. Can't wear armor, but can use shields. This means a Tortle with a shield has AC 19 with no stat investment.
  - **Shell Defense**: As an action, withdraw into shell. While withdrawn: +4 AC, advantage on STR and CON saves, disadvantage on DEX saves, speed is 0, can't take reactions, only action available is to emerge (bonus action). Prone, incapacitated, and can't benefit from speed increases.
  - **Nature's Intuition**: Proficiency in one of: Animal Handling, Medicine, Nature, Perception, Survival.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

17 flat AC is the standout. Strongest on MAD classes that can't afford DEX (Monk, some Clerics).

### Triton

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium
- **Speed**: 30 ft walking, 30 ft swimming
- **Traits**:
  - **Amphibious**: Breathe air and water.
  - **Control Air and Water**: Cast Fog Cloud once per long rest without a spell slot. At 3rd level, cast Gust of Wind. At 5th level, cast Water Walk. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Emissary of the Sea**: Can communicate simple ideas with beasts, elementals, and monstrosities that have a swimming speed.
  - **Guardian of the Depths**: Resistance to cold damage. Ignore drawbacks of deep underwater environments.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

### Yuan-ti

- **Type**: Humanoid
- **Size**: Medium or Small
- **Speed**: 30 ft
- **Traits**:
  - **Darkvision**: 60 ft
  - **Magic Resistance**: Advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects. Same as Satyr — one of the best racial traits.
  - **Poison Resilience**: Resistance to poison damage. Advantage on saves against the poisoned condition.
  - **Serpentine Spellcasting**: Know Poison Spray cantrip. At 3rd level, cast Animal Friendship (targeting snakes only) at will without a spell slot. At 5th level, cast Suggestion once per long rest without a spell slot. Also castable with spell slots. Casting ability: INT, WIS, or CHA.
- **Languages**: Common + one other

Magic Resistance + Poison Resilience makes Yuan-ti mechanically strong. Same Magic Resistance concern as Satyr.
