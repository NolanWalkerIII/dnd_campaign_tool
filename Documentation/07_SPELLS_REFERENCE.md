# Spells Reference

Organized by level. Each entry includes: name, school, casting time, range, components, duration, classes, and a concise mechanical description. This covers the most commonly used SRD spells — not an exhaustive list, but enough for an LLM to run most situations.

For spellcasting rules, see `06_SPELLCASTING.md`.

---

## Cantrips (Level 0)

**Blade Ward** | Abjuration | 1 action | Self | V, S | 1 round
Resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, slashing damage from weapon attacks until end of next turn. Rarely worth your action.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Chill Touch** | Necromancy | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | 1 round
Ranged spell attack, 1d8 necrotic. Target can't regain HP until start of your next turn. Against undead: also disadvantage on attacks against you. Scales at 5/11/17.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Eldritch Blast** | Evocation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | Instant
Ranged spell attack, 1d10 force. Extra beams at 5 (2), 11 (3), 17 (4). Each beam targets separately. The Warlock's bread and butter.
*Classes: Warlock*

**Fire Bolt** | Evocation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | Instant
Ranged spell attack, 1d10 fire. Ignites unattended flammable objects. Scales at 5/11/17.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Guidance** | Divination | 1 action | Touch | V, S | Concentration, 1 min
Target adds 1d4 to one ability check of their choice before the spell ends. The most-cast cantrip in the game.
*Classes: Cleric, Druid*

**Light** | Evocation | 1 action | Touch | V, M (firefly or phosphorescent moss) | 1 hour
Object sheds bright light 20 ft, dim 20 ft. Only one Light at a time.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Mage Hand** | Conjuration | 1 action | 30 ft | V, S | 1 min
Spectral hand. Can manipulate objects, open doors, retrieve items, pour vials. 10 lb limit. Can't attack or activate magic items. AT Rogue version can be invisible and use thieves' tools.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Mending** | Transmutation | 1 minute | Touch | V, S, M | Instant
Repair a single break or tear in an object (torn cloak, broken chain link, cracked vial). Max 1 ft in any dimension.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Message** | Transmutation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S, M | 1 round
Whisper to a target within range. They hear it and can reply in a whisper. Line of effect (but thin barriers don't block it — 1 ft of stone, 1 inch of metal, or 3 ft of wood blocks).
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Minor Illusion** | Illusion | 1 action | 30 ft | S, M | 1 minute
Create a sound OR an image (not both). Image is static, 5 ft cube. Investigation check vs. spell DC to discern illusion. Physical interaction reveals it.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Prestidigitation** | Transmutation | 1 action | 10 ft | V, S | Up to 1 hour
Minor magical tricks: light/snuff a small flame, clean/soil a small object, chill/warm/flavor material, create a small trinket or illusion for 1 round. Up to 3 non-instantaneous effects at once.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Ray of Frost** | Evocation | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S | Instant
Ranged spell attack, 1d8 cold. Target's speed reduced by 10 ft until start of your next turn. Scales at 5/11/17.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Sacred Flame** | Evocation | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S | Instant
DEX save or 1d8 radiant. Ignores cover. Scales at 5/11/17.
*Classes: Cleric*

**Spare the Dying** | Necromancy | 1 action | Touch | V, S | Instant
Stabilize a creature at 0 HP. Replaces a DC 10 Medicine check.
*Classes: Cleric*

**Thaumaturgy** | Transmutation | 1 action | 30 ft | V | Up to 1 minute
Minor divine tricks: boom your voice, cause flames to flicker/change color, cause tremors, open/slam doors/windows, alter eye appearance. Up to 3 effects at once.
*Classes: Cleric*

**Toll the Dead** | Necromancy | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S | Instant
WIS save or 1d8 necrotic (1d12 if target is missing any HP). Scales at 5/11/17. Best cleric damage cantrip.
*Classes: Cleric, Warlock, Wizard*

**Vicious Mockery** | Enchantment | 1 action | 60 ft | V | Instant
WIS save or 1d4 psychic + disadvantage on next attack before end of next turn. The damage is bad; the disadvantage is the point.
*Classes: Bard*

## 1st Level

**Bless** | Enchantment | 1 action | 30 ft | V, S, M (holy water) | Concentration, 1 min
Up to 3 creatures add 1d4 to attack rolls and saving throws. One of the best 1st-level concentration spells in the game.
*Classes: Cleric, Paladin*

**Burning Hands** | Evocation | 1 action | Self (15 ft cone) | V, S | Instant
3d6 fire, DEX save half. Ignites flammable objects.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Charm Person** | Enchantment | 1 action | 30 ft | V, S | 1 hour
WIS save (advantage if you're fighting them). On fail: charmed by you. Knows it was charmed when spell ends. +1 target per slot level above 1st.
*Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Cure Wounds** | Evocation | 1 action | Touch | V, S | Instant
1d8 + casting mod HP restored. +1d8 per slot above 1st.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger*

**Detect Magic** | Divination (Ritual) | 1 action | Self | V, S | Concentration, 10 min
Sense magic within 30 ft. Use action to see aura and determine school. Blocked by 1 ft stone, 1 inch metal, 3 ft wood, thin sheet of lead.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Disguise Self** | Illusion | 1 action | Self | V, S | 1 hour
Change your appearance (including clothes, armor, weapons, belongings). Can seem 1 ft shorter/taller. Physical inspection + Investigation vs. spell DC reveals it.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Faerie Fire** | Evocation | 1 action | 60 ft | V | Concentration, 1 min
20 ft cube. DEX save or outlined in light — no benefit from invisibility, attacks against have advantage. Strong anti-invisible and damage-boost spell.
*Classes: Bard, Druid*

**Healing Word** | Evocation | 1 bonus action | 60 ft | V | Instant
1d4 + casting mod HP. Bonus action + range = the best emergency heal. +1d4 per slot above 1st.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid*

**Hex** | Enchantment | 1 bonus action | 90 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 hour
+1d6 necrotic on each hit. Choose ability — target has disadvantage on checks with that ability. If target dies, bonus action to move to new target. Duration increases with slot level.
*Classes: Warlock*

**Hunter's Mark** | Divination | 1 bonus action | 90 ft | V | Concentration, 1 hour
+1d6 on each weapon hit. Advantage on Perception/Survival to find target. Transfer to new target as bonus action when target dies.
*Classes: Ranger*

**Identify** | Divination (Ritual) | 1 minute | Touch | V, S, M (pearl worth 100 gp) | Instant
Learn properties of a magic item or spells affecting a creature/object. Doesn't reveal curses (debatable per DM).
*Classes: Bard, Wizard*

**Magic Missile** | Evocation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | Instant
Three darts, 1d4+1 force each. Auto-hit (no attack roll). Each dart can target separately. Blocked by Shield spell. +1 dart per slot above 1st.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Shield** | Abjuration | 1 reaction (when hit by attack or targeted by Magic Missile) | Self | V, S | 1 round
+5 AC until start of next turn, including against the triggering attack. Immune to Magic Missile. Arguably the most important defensive spell in the game.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Shield of Faith** | Abjuration | 1 bonus action | 60 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 10 min
+2 AC to one creature. Solid sustained defense.
*Classes: Cleric, Paladin*

**Sleep** | Enchantment | 1 action | 90 ft | V, S, M | 1 minute
5d8 HP of creatures fall asleep (starting with lowest current HP). No save. Doesn't affect undead or creatures immune to charmed. Devastating at level 1, useless by level 5. +2d8 per slot above 1st.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Thunderwave** | Evocation | 1 action | Self (15 ft cube) | V, S | Instant
2d8 thunder, CON save half. On fail: pushed 10 ft. Audible 300 ft. Good for getting out of melee.
*Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard*

## 2nd Level

**Hold Person** | Enchantment | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 min
WIS save or paralyzed (save at end of each turn). Humanoids only. Melee attacks against paralyzed = auto-crit. Encounter-ending if it lands. +1 target per slot above 2nd.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Lesser Restoration** | Abjuration | 1 action | Touch | V, S | Instant
End one disease or condition: blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or poisoned.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Ranger*

**Misty Step** | Conjuration | 1 bonus action | Self | V | Instant
Teleport 30 ft to an unoccupied space you can see. Bonus action. No verbal-only = hard to counter.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Moonbeam** | Evocation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 min
5 ft radius, 40 ft high cylinder. 2d10 radiant, CON save half. Damages on entering and when starting turn in area. Move cylinder 60 ft as action. Shapechangers make save with disadvantage and revert on fail.
*Classes: Druid*

**Pass Without Trace** | Abjuration | 1 action | Self | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 hour
You and allies within 30 ft get +10 to Stealth. Can't be tracked except by magical means. Makes the whole party effectively invisible in many situations.
*Classes: Druid, Ranger*

**Spiritual Weapon** | Evocation | 1 bonus action | 60 ft | V, S | 1 minute
Create floating weapon. Bonus action to attack: melee spell attack, 1d8 + casting mod force. Move 20 ft per bonus action. No concentration. The Cleric's free damage engine.
*Classes: Cleric*

**Web** | Conjuration | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 hour
20 ft cube. DEX save or restrained. Restrained creature can use action to make STR check vs. spell DC to break free. Difficult terrain. Flammable (2d4 fire per round).
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*

## 3rd Level

**Counterspell** | Abjuration | 1 reaction | 60 ft | S | Instant
Counter a spell being cast. Auto-success if target spell is 3rd level or lower. For higher: ability check DC = 10 + spell level. Using a higher slot auto-counters that level and below.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Dispel Magic** | Abjuration | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | Instant
End one spell on a target creature, object, or area. Auto for 3rd level or lower. Higher: ability check DC = 10 + spell level. Higher slot = auto-dispel that level and below.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Fireball** | Evocation | 1 action | 150 ft | V, S, M (tiny ball of bat guano and sulfur) | Instant
20 ft radius sphere. 8d6 fire, DEX save half. Ignites flammable objects. Spreads around corners. +1d6 per slot above 3rd.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Fly** | Transmutation | 1 action | Touch | V, S, M | Concentration, 10 min
Fly speed 60 ft. If concentration ends, target falls. +1 target per slot above 3rd.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Haste** | Transmutation | 1 action | 30 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 min
Double speed, +2 AC, advantage on DEX saves, additional action (Attack [one attack only], Dash, Disengage, Hide, Use an Object). When spell ends: can't move or take actions until after next turn. The lethargy clause makes losing concentration painful.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Lightning Bolt** | Evocation | 1 action | Self (100 ft line) | V, S, M | Instant
5 ft wide, 100 ft long line. 8d6 lightning, DEX save half. +1d6 per slot above 3rd.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Revivify** | Necromancy | 1 action | Touch | V, S, M (diamonds worth 300 gp, consumed) | Instant
Creature that died within 1 minute returns with 1 HP. Doesn't restore missing body parts. First resurrection spell available — Clerics get it at level 5.
*Classes: Cleric, Paladin*

**Spirit Guardians** | Conjuration | 1 action | Self (15 ft radius) | V, S, M | Concentration, 10 min
Spirits surround you. Hostile creatures entering or starting their turn in the area: WIS save or 3d8 radiant/necrotic (half on save). Area is difficult terrain for enemies. The Cleric's primary AOE damage spell.
*Classes: Cleric*

## 4th Level

**Banishment** | Abjuration | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 min
CHA save or banished to harmless demiplane. If native to another plane and you maintain full concentration: permanently sent back to their home plane. +1 target per slot above 4th.
*Classes: Cleric, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Dimension Door** | Conjuration | 1 action | 500 ft | V | Instant
Teleport to a point within 500 ft. Can bring one willing creature of your size or smaller. If you'd arrive in an occupied space: 4d6 force damage and spell fails.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard*

**Greater Invisibility** | Illusion | 1 action | Touch | V, S | Concentration, 1 min
Invisible for the duration. Attacking and casting don't break it (unlike regular Invisibility).
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Polymorph** | Transmutation | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 1 hour
Transform creature into a beast of CR ≤ target's level (or CR for a monster). New form's HP replaces current. When new form drops to 0, revert with excess damage carrying over. Great for temp HP (Giant Ape, T-Rex) or removing an enemy from the fight (turn them into a snail).
*Classes: Bard, Druid, Sorcerer, Wizard*

## 5th Level

**Animate Objects** | Transmutation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S | Concentration, 1 min
Animate up to 10 nonmagical objects. Each acts on your turn. Tiny objects: +8 to hit, 1d4+4 damage, AC 18, 20 HP. 10 tiny objects = huge damage output.
*Classes: Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard*

**Greater Restoration** | Abjuration | 1 action | Touch | V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100 gp, consumed) | Instant
End one of: a charm/petrification, a curse, any ability score reduction, any HP max reduction, or one level of exhaustion.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Druid*

**Raise Dead** | Necromancy | 1 hour | Touch | V, S, M (diamond worth 500 gp, consumed) | Instant
Return a creature dead no more than 10 days. Returns with 1 HP. -4 penalty to attacks, saves, and ability checks, reduced by 1 per long rest. Doesn't work if creature died of old age. Missing vital body parts = spell fails.
*Classes: Bard, Cleric, Paladin*

**Wall of Force** | Evocation | 1 action | 120 ft | V, S, M | Concentration, 10 min
Invisible wall. Ten 10 ft × 10 ft panels (or dome/sphere). Nothing physical or magical passes through. Can't be dispelled by Dispel Magic. Disintegrate destroys it. Arguably the best control spell in the game.
*Classes: Wizard*

## 6th-9th Level (Key Spells)

**Heal** (6th) | Evocation | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S | Instant
Target regains 70 HP. Cures blindness, deafness, and any diseases.
*Classes: Cleric, Druid*

**Mass Heal** (9th) | Evocation | 1 action | 60 ft | V, S | Instant
Restore up to 700 HP total divided among creatures in range. Cures all diseases, blindness, deafness.
*Classes: Cleric*

**True Resurrection** (9th) | Necromancy | 1 hour | Touch | V, S, M (diamonds worth 25,000 gp, consumed) | Instant
Restore creature dead up to 200 years. Provides a new body if needed. Full HP, no penalties.
*Classes: Cleric, Druid*

**Wish** (9th) | Conjuration | 1 action | Self | V | Instant
The most powerful spell. Replicate any 8th-level or lower spell with no components. Beyond that: DM adjudicates. After a non-replication use: 33% chance of never casting Wish again, 1d10 necrotic per spell level cast until long rest, STR drops to 3 for 2d4 days.
*Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard*
