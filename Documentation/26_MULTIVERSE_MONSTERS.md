# Multiverse Monsters Reference

Monsters from Mordenkainen Presents: Monsters of the Multiverse (MotM), Xanathar's Guide to Everything (encounter tables), and other expansion sources. This supplements the core Monster Guide (`11_MONSTER_GUIDE.md`).

---

## MotM Changes to Monsters

MotM revised ~250 monster stat blocks from Volo's Guide to Monsters and Mordenkainen's Tome of Foes. Key changes:
- **Spellcasting simplified**: Most monsters lost traditional spell lists. Instead they get "Spellcasting" as a trait with a curated short list, or spell-like actions (e.g., "Psychic Blast (Recharge 5-6)" instead of casting Mind Blast as a spell)
- **Bonus actions added**: Many monsters now have bonus action abilities for better action economy
- **Proficiency bonus (PB)** listed explicitly in stat blocks
- **Creature types updated**: Some creatures changed types
- **CR adjustments**: Some monsters got CR changes

These revisions make monsters easier to run — fewer spell lists to track, more streamlined actions.

## Notable MotM Monsters by CR

### CR 0-2 (Low Tier)

**Chitine** (CR 1/2, Monstrosity)
- AC 14 (hide), HP 18, Speed 30 ft, climb 30 ft
- Multiattack: 3 dagger attacks (+5, 1d4+3 piercing each)
- Web Sense: knows location of any creature in contact with same web
- Web Walker: ignores web movement restrictions
- Fey Ancestry: advantage on saves vs. charmed
- *Good for: Underdark encounters, Lolth-themed campaigns*

**Vegepygmy** (CR 1/4, Plant)
- AC 13, HP 13, Speed 30 ft
- Claws: +5 to hit, 1d6+3 slashing
- Plant Camouflage: advantage on Stealth in terrain with ample plant life
- Regeneration: regains 3 HP at start of turn
- *Good for: swamp/jungle encounters, Zuggtmoy themes*

**Duergar** variants (CR 1-2)
- Multiple variants: Duergar Mind Master (CR 2), Duergar Stone Guard (CR 2), Duergar Kavalrachni (CR 2, mounted on female steeder)
- All share Sunlight Sensitivity and Duergar Resilience
- Mind Master has Mind Mastery (charm/dominate), Stone Guard has Phalanx Formation

### CR 3-5 (Adventurer Tier)

**Choldrith** (CR 3, Monstrosity)
- AC 15, HP 66, Speed 30 ft, climb 30 ft
- Spellcasting (WIS, DC 12): Guidance, Thaumaturgy, Bane, Healing Word, Hold Person, Spiritual Weapon
- Web (Recharge 5-6): restrain in webbing, DC 12 STR to escape
- Dagger + Fangs multiattack with poison (CON save or poisoned 1 min)
- *Good for: spider-themed dungeons, Lolth priestess encounters*

**Neogi** (CR 3, Aberration)
- AC 15, HP 33, Speed 30 ft, climb 30 ft
- Multiattack: bite (1d6+3 + 1d6 poison) + claws (1d8+1)
- Enslave (Recharge 6): WIS DC 14 save or charmed 24 hours. Can repeat only when damaged
- Mental Fortitude: advantage on saves vs. charmed/frightened
- *Good for: Spelljammer/astral encounters, slaver villains*

**Vampiric Mist** (CR 3, Undead)
- AC 13, HP 30, Speed 0 ft, fly 30 ft (hover)
- Life Drain: 10-ft radius, CON DC 13, 2d6 necrotic on fail, mist regains that many HP
- Misty Form: can move through spaces as narrow as 1 inch
- Sunlight Hypersensitivity: takes 10 radiant per turn in sunlight
- Forbiddance: can't enter residence without invitation
- *Good for: gothic horror, vampire-adjacent encounters*

**Tlincalli** (CR 5, Monstrosity)
- AC 15, HP 85, Speed 40 ft
- Multiattack: longsword + sting
- Sting: +6, 1d6+3 piercing + 4d6 poison (CON DC 14 half, also poisoned 1 min on fail)
- Restrain: can grapple with sting and restrain (escape DC 14)
- *Good for: desert encounters, scorpion-folk*

### CR 6-10 (Mid Tier)

**Annis Hag** (CR 6, Fey)
- AC 17 (natural), HP 90, Speed 40 ft
- Multiattack: bite + 2 claws. Bite: +7, 3d6+4 piercing. Claws: +7, 3d6+4 slashing
- Crushing Hug: grapple + 9d6+4 bludgeoning (escape DC 15)
- Spellcasting (CHA, DC 15): Disguise Self (at will), Fog Cloud
- Iron Token: create up to 3 iron tokens — bearer can communicate telepathically with hag
- *Good for: hag coven encounters, dark fairy tale themes*

**Drow Shadowblade** (CR 6, Humanoid)
- AC 17 (studded leather), HP 71, Speed 30 ft
- Shadow Sword: +7, 2d6+4 psychic + 3d6 necrotic
- Shadow Step: bonus action teleport 30 ft between dim light/darkness
- Spellcasting: Darkness, at will
- Summon Shadow Demon: once per day
- *Good for: Underdark assassin encounters*

**Korred** (CR 7, Fey)
- AC 17 (natural), HP 102, Speed 30 ft, burrow 30 ft
- Multiattack: 2 greatclub attacks (+9, 2d8+6 bludgeoning)
- Command Hair: 30-ft range, bonus action, acts as animate rope/grapple (STR DC 13)
- Stone Stride: can teleport between stone surfaces within 30 ft
- Spellcasting (WIS, DC 13): Commune with Nature, Meld into Stone, Stone Shape, Conjure Elemental (earth only), Otto's Irresistible Dance, Stoneskin
- *Good for: fey-themed, mountain/cave encounters*

**Mind Flayer** (CR 7, Aberration)
- AC 15, HP 71, Speed 30 ft
- Tentacles: +7, 2d10+4 psychic. If target is stunned, auto-grapple (escape DC 15). If grappled at start of flayer's turn: extract brain (instant kill if target is incapacitated and has a brain, reduces INT to 0)
- Mind Blast (Recharge 5-6): 60-ft cone, INT DC 15, 4d8+4 psychic + stunned 1 min (save at end of each turn). This is the encounter-defining ability.
- Spellcasting (INT, DC 15): Detect Thoughts, Levitate (at will), Dominate Monster, Plane Shift (self only)
- Magic Resistance: advantage on saves vs. spells
- Telepathy 120 ft
- *One of the most iconic and dangerous CR 7 monsters. The stun → extract brain combo is lethal.*

**Drow Arachnomancer** (CR 8, Humanoid)
- AC 15, HP 162, Speed 30 ft, climb 30 ft
- Multiattack: bite + poison staff or 2 poison sprays
- Spider Form: bonus action to change into Giant Spider (or back). Can speak in spider form.
- Spellcasting (INT/WIS, DC 15): Dancing Lights, Darkness, Faerie Fire, Shield, Dispel Magic, Etherealness, Cloudkill

**Howler** (CR 8, Fiend)
- AC 16, HP 90, Speed 40 ft
- Rending Bite: +8, 2d6+5 piercing + 4d10 psychic
- Mind-Breaking Howl (Recharge 4-6): each creature within 60 ft makes WIS DC 16 or frightened until end of next turn. If already frightened: stunned instead
- Pack Tactics: advantage on attacks if ally within 5 ft
- *Good for: Pandemonium/lower planes encounters*

**Gray Render** (CR 8, Monstrosity)
- AC 19 (natural), HP 189, Speed 30 ft
- Multiattack: bite (2d12+7) + 2 claws (2d8+7)
- Bloody Rampage: when reduced to half HP, can make a bite attack as a reaction
- *189 HP and 19 AC at CR 8 is beefy. Simple but dangerous bruiser.*

### CR 11-15 (High Tier)

**Alhoon** (CR 10, Undead)
- Basically a mind flayer lich
- Mind Blast (Recharge 5-6): 60-ft cone, INT DC 16, 5d8+5 psychic + stunned
- Spellcasting (INT, DC 16): Detect Magic, Disguise Self, Mage Hand (at will), Counterspell, Lightning Bolt, Wall of Force, Modify Memory
- Negate Turning: undead within 30 ft have advantage on saves vs. Turn Undead
- *Mind flayer with lich-level spellcasting. Very dangerous.*

**Drow Matron Mother** (CR 12, Humanoid)
- AC 17, HP 247, Speed 30 ft
- Demon Staff: +10, 1d6+4 bludgeoning + 4d6 poison
- Multiattack: 3 attacks (staff, tentacle rod combo)
- Spellcasting (WIS, DC 17): Command, Banishment, Blade Barrier, Dispel Magic, Darkness, Gate, Heal, 13+ spells total
- Lolth's Fickle Favor: bonus action, bestow advantage or disadvantage on one creature within 30 ft
- Summon Demon: once per day, summon a Yochlol or Retriever
- *Boss-level Drow encounter. Bring dispel magic.*

**Elder Brain** (CR 14, Aberration)
- AC 10, HP 210, Speed 5 ft, swim 10 ft
- Tentacle: +7, 4d8+4 psychic. On hit, target is grappled (escape DC 15). Grappled target is stunned.
- Mind Blast (Recharge 5-6): 60-ft cone, INT DC 18, 5d10+5 psychic + stunned 1 min
- Psychic Link: bonus action, sense up to 10 creatures within 2 miles (through Mind Flayer intermediaries)
- Spellcasting (INT, DC 18): Detect Thoughts (at will), Dominate Monster, Plane Shift (self only)
- Legendary Actions (3): Tentacle, Break Concentration (target repeats concentration save), Psychic Pulse (2 actions, 4d8 psychic in 30-ft radius, INT save DC 18)
- Creature Sense: knows location and INT score of every creature within 5 miles
- *The boss of a mind flayer colony. Fights from a pool. Usually has mind flayer bodyguards.*

**Nagpa** (CR 17, Monstrosity)
- AC 18, HP 187, Speed 30 ft
- Staff: +9, 1d6+4 bludgeoning + 5d6 necrotic
- Spellcasting (INT, DC 17): Mage Hand, Message, Minor Illusion (at will), Fireball, Hold Person, Counterspell, Wall of Fire, Dominate Person, Feeblemind, Finger of Death
- Paralysis (Recharge 6): one creature within 30 ft, CON DC 17, or paralyzed 1 min (save at end of each turn)
- Corruption: bonus action, one creature within 30 ft, WIS DC 17, or use its reaction to make a weapon attack against a target of the Nagpa's choice
- *Ancient cursed wizards. Strong spellcaster with nasty control abilities.*

### CR 16-20 (Endgame Tier)

**Star Spawn Larva Mage** (CR 16, Aberration)
- AC 16, HP 236, Speed 30 ft
- Slam: +11, 3d8+5 bludgeoning + 3d6 psychic
- Plague of Worms (Recharge 6): 120-ft line or 30-ft cone, DEX DC 19, 12d6 necrotic (half on save), and a swarm of insects appears that attacks
- Spellcasting (CHA, DC 17): At will: Eldritch Blast (4 beams), Mage Hand. Dominate Monster, Fly, Counterspell, Evard's Black Tentacles, Hunger of Hadar
- Return to Worms: when reduced to 0 HP, explodes (DEX DC 19, 12d6 necrotic in 10 ft) and reforms with 1 HP at the start of its next turn
- *Has to be reduced to 0 HP then dealt with while reforming, or it keeps coming back.*

**Sibriex** (CR 18, Fiend)
- AC 17, HP 150, Speed 0 ft, fly 20 ft (hover)
- Multiattack: chain + 3 bites or 3 squirts
- Warp Creature: 120-ft range, CON DC 20 save. On fail, target is poisoned. Poisoned target makes CON save at start of each turn — on fail, body is warped and deformed (disadvantage on attacks and ability checks). Three fails = permanently transformed (requires Wish to reverse). Brutal save-or-suck ability.
- Contamination: creatures within 30 ft make CON DC 20 or poisoned
- Spellcasting (CHA, DC 21): at will — Command, Hold Monster, Charm Monster, Feeblemind
- *Demonic flesh-crafting horror. The Warp Creature ability is nightmare fuel.*

**Elder Tempest** (CR 23, Elemental)
- AC 19, HP 264, Speed 0 ft, fly 120 ft (hover)
- Gargantuan size. Living storm.
- Thunderous Slam: +16, 4d12+9 bludgeoning + 2d12 thunder
- Lightning Storm: each creature within 120 ft makes DEX DC 21 or takes 3d12 lightning. Creatures within 30 ft auto-take 2d12 thunder at start of Elder Tempest's turn.
- Legendary Actions (3): Move, Lightning Strike (one creature, DEX DC 21, 3d12 lightning), Screaming Gale (2 actions, 60-ft cone, creatures pushed 60 ft and prone, STR DC 21 to halve and stay standing)
- *Walking (flying) natural disaster. Use for world-threat encounters.*

## Iconic MotM Monster Groups

### Devils (Expanded)

**Merregon** (CR 4): Foot soldiers of Avernus. AC 16, HP 45. Shield Wall reaction — nearby merregons share AC. Pack tactics with other devils.

**Orthon** (CR 10): Devil bounty hunters. Invisibility field, infernal crossbow (2d10+6 + 4d10 fire + 3d10 poison). Explosive trap reaction when it dies.

**Amnizu** (CR 18): Devil taskmasters. Forgetfulness (INT DC 18 — forget everything from past 5 hours). Poison stinger. Heavy spellcasting. 202 HP, AC 21.

### Demons (Expanded)

**Babau** (CR 4): Demon assassins. Weakening Gaze (CON DC 13 or disadvantage on attacks/saves for 1 min). Innate Darkness. 82 HP.

**Dybbuk** (CR 4): Possess corpses. Can control a dead humanoid body and access its memories.

**Alkilith** (CR 11): Fungal demon that turns doorways into portals to the Abyss. 157 HP, AC 17. Its presence corrupts doors and gates.

**Molydeus** (CR 21): Two-headed demon enforcers. 216 HP, AC 19. Snakebite: CON DC 22 or 6d8 + target's max HP reduced by that amount (save or die territory on low HP targets). Legendary Actions.

### Mind Flayers (Expanded)

**Mind Flayer Psion** (CR 8): Mind flayer + expanded psionics (Dominate Monster, Telekinesis, Wall of Force).

**Ulitharid** (CR 9): Six-tentacle elite mind flayer. Extract Brain ability like standard flayer. Mind Blast DC 17, 5d8+5. Can create new Elder Brains.

### Shadar-kai (Expanded)

**Shadar-kai Shadow Dancer** (CR 7): Shadow Jump 30 ft bonus action (dim/dark). Shadow Step — if in dim light, can make opportunity attacks against creatures 30 ft away.

**Shadar-kai Gloom Weaver** (CR 9): Spellcaster. Burden of Time — aging touch (CON DC 16, 6d6 necrotic + no reactions until next turn). Hypnotic Pattern, Greater Invisibility.

**Shadar-kai Soul Monger** (CR 11): Soul Rip — 60-ft cone, CON DC 16, 5d8 necrotic + max HP reduced by damage dealt (lasts until long rest). Weight of Ages — aging aura, advantage for allies, disadvantage for enemies in 30 ft. 136 HP.
