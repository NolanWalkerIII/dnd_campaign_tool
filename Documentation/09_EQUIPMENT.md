### Weapon Properties

- **Ammunition**: Requires ammo. Recover half spent ammo after combat (1 min searching).
- **Finesse**: Use STR or DEX for attack and damage.
- **Heavy**: Small creatures have disadvantage.
- **Light**: Can be used for two-weapon fighting.
- **Loading**: One attack per action/bonus action/reaction regardless of Extra Attack. Crossbow Expert feat removes this.
- **Reach**: +5 ft melee reach (10 ft total).
- **Special**: See weapon description.
- **Thrown**: Can throw for ranged attack using same ability modifier as melee.
- **Two-Handed**: Requires both hands.
- **Versatile**: Can be used one-handed or two-handed (two-handed damage in parentheses).

## Armor

### Light Armor (DEX mod added to AC)

| Armor | AC | STR Req | Stealth | Cost | Weight |
|-------|-----|---------|---------|------|--------|
| Padded | 11 + DEX | — | Disadvantage | 5 gp | 8 lb |
| Leather | 11 + DEX | — | — | 10 gp | 10 lb |
| Studded Leather | 12 + DEX | — | — | 45 gp | 13 lb |

Studded leather is the best light armor. No reason to use padded.

### Medium Armor (DEX mod added, max +2)

| Armor | AC | STR Req | Stealth | Cost | Weight |
|-------|-----|---------|---------|------|--------|
| Hide | 12 + DEX (max 2) | — | — | 10 gp | 12 lb |
| Chain Shirt | 13 + DEX (max 2) | — | — | 50 gp | 20 lb |
| Scale Mail | 14 + DEX (max 2) | — | Disadvantage | 50 gp | 45 lb |
| Breastplate | 14 + DEX (max 2) | — | — | 400 gp | 20 lb |
| Half Plate | 15 + DEX (max 2) | — | Disadvantage | 750 gp | 40 lb |

Breastplate is the go-to for stealth builds (AC 16 with +2 DEX, no stealth penalty). Half plate is strictly better AC but kills stealth. Medium Armor Master feat removes the stealth disadvantage and raises the DEX cap to +3.

## Carrying Capacity

```
Carrying capacity = STR score × 15 (in pounds)
Push/drag/lift = STR score × 30
```

### Encumbrance (Optional Rule)
- Over 5 × STR: speed -10 ft
- Over 10 × STR: speed -20 ft, disadvantage on ability checks, attacks, and saves using STR, DEX, or CON

### Size Modifiers
- Tiny: × 0.5
- Small/Medium: × 1
- Large: × 2
- Huge: × 4
- Gargantuan: × 8
