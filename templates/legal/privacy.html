Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

What We Collect

Campaign Codex ("we", "us") collects the following information when you use our service:

  • Account information: Username and password (hashed)
  • Phone number: If you opt in to SMS Play Mode, your mobile phone number
  • Game data: Character sheets, campaign data, and in-game messages

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the D&D campaign management and SMS play service
  • To send game-related SMS messages (actions, narration, notifications) when you opt in
  • To process your in-game actions through our AI Dungeon Master

SMS Messaging

When a Dungeon Master registers your phone number for SMS Play Mode:

  • You will receive a verification SMS asking you to reply with the campaign join code
  • By replying with the code, you consent to receive game-related messages
  • Message frequency varies based on gameplay (typically 1-20 messages per session)
  • Message and data rates may apply
  • Text STOP at any time to opt out of SMS messages
  • Text HELP for support information

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is shared only with:

  • Twilio: To deliver SMS messages (phone number and message content)
  • xAI: To process game actions through our AI Dungeon Master (game context only, no personal data)

Data Retention & Deletion

Game data is retained for the duration of your account. A Dungeon Master can unlink your phone number at any time, and you can text STOP to immediately opt out of SMS messages. Contact us to request account deletion.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: john@jsbjr.digital
